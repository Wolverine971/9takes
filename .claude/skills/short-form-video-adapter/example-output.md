# Short-Form Video Pack: Pete Davidson

## Concept Options

1. Tattoo removal as conflict avoidance
2. Why self-deprecation makes him hard to read
3. The public-chaos/private-softness split

## Recommended Direction

- Tattoo removal as conflict avoidance gives the cleanest scene, strongest proof, and clearest debate prompt.

## Hooks

- Pete Davidson spending $200k removing tattoos is not random, it's a personality tell.
- Most people saw a style reset. I see avoidance.
- The tattoo removal story makes more sense when you stop reading it as aesthetics.

## Script (30-60s)

0-2s: state the tattoo-removal payoff
3-10s: show the concrete fact and scene
11-25s: explain the interpretation
26-40s: stack 2 supporting behavior examples
41-55s: add boundary and counter-signal
56-60s: ask agree/disagree CTA

## On-Screen Text

- "$200k tattoo removal"
- "style reset or avoidance?"
- "pattern > aesthetics"

## Visual Plan

- talking head: direct camera delivery
- b-roll: tattoo photos, interview stills, relationship headlines
- captions emphasis: highlight the concrete numbers and pattern words

## Caption

Most people read this as rebranding. I think it also reads like emotional reset behavior. Different motive, different prediction.

## CTA

- Agree or disagree, is this reinvention or avoidance?

## Disclaimer / Boundary

- Public-data-based interpretation only, not a diagnosis.
