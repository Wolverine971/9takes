## 9takes translation

"**Your takes are boring.**" Four "you're…" callouts:

- **You're saying what everyone already thinks.** The first reaction is the least revealing one.
- **You're judging, not reading.** The interesting part is _why_ they did it.
- **You're projecting your own motive.** Same act, nine reasons.
- **You're playing it safe.** The take that costs you something is the one people remember.
  CTA: "Stop performing. Comment your real take." This is the sharpest engagement format in the whole set for 9takes — second-person + respectfully provocative is squarely on-brand, and it feeds the give-first wall (boldness over polish). Run it as a pair with an "Anti-Boring Takes" solution deck.
