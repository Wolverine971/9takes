## Key Insights

### What's Working

1. **Mental health authority established** — #1 for "enneagram mental illness"
2. **Manipulation post ranking quickly** — #2 after only ~24 days
3. **Multiple pages ranking for same query** — Shows domain authority building
4. **Shadow/problem content strategy validated** — These queries drive traffic

### What Needs Improvement

1. **New December posts need more time** — ADHD, self-sabotage, overthinking not ranking yet
2. **Competition from established sites** — Psychology Junkie, Truity, Personality Growth
3. **Long-tail keywords** — Need more specific targeting

### Recommended Actions

1. **Wait until mid-January** — Give Google more time to index December content
2. **Add internal links** — Connect new posts to ranking power pages
3. **Build backlinks** — Guest posts, social sharing, community engagement
4. **Monitor competitors** — Track what Psychology Junkie and Truity publish
5. **Test AI platforms directly** — Manual testing needed for ChatGPT/Perplexity
