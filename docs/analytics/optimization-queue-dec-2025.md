## Key Insights

### What's Working

1. **Mental health content** - enneagram-and-mental-illness is crushing it (5.33% CTR)
2. **Shadow/toxic content** - toxic-traits post has 4.1% CTR
3. **Problem-focused titles** - Anything with "why" or problem framing converts
4. **Cross-system content** - Astrology comparison driving traffic

### What's NOT Working

1. **Generic celebrity pages** - Huge impressions, terrible CTR (<0.5%)
2. **Type description pages** - Getting impressions but not converting
3. **Position issues** - Many pages rank 8-15, need to push to top 5

### Biggest Opportunities

1. **IShowSpeed** - 22K impressions, 0.16% CTR. Even 1% CTR = 220 clicks
2. **Celebrity mental health angles** - Connect to proven content type
3. **Type pages** - Need Quick Answer boxes and problem framing
4. **Query matching** - Many searches don't match our titles
