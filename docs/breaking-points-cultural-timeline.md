## Analysis Framework for Each Event

### Questions to Answer for Each Major Event:

1. **Timing:** How soon after the event did Breaking Points cover it?
2. **Krystal's Angle (Type 1):** Moral/principled stance? Righteous anger? Structural critique?
3. **Saagar's Angle (Type 7):** Systems analysis? Intellectual curiosity? Historical context?
4. **Their Dynamic:** Did they agree or disagree? How did they handle disagreement?
5. **vs. Mainstream:** How was their coverage different from CNN/MSNBC/Fox?
6. **Audience Reaction:** Comments, engagement, controversy?
7. **Consistency:** Did their reactions align with their stated types/patterns?

### Key Patterns to Track:

- **Krystal's Type 1 Triggers:** Injustice, hypocrisy, corporate capture, working-class betrayal
- **Saagar's Type 7 Patterns:** Historical parallels, geopolitical context, intellectual rabbit holes
- **1-7 Dynamic:** Who grounded the conversation? Who expanded it?
- **Anti-Establishment Consistency:** Did they maintain their "hate the ruling class" stance?
