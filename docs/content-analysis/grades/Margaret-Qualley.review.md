FEEDBACK (2026-07-23):

- The cold-open misdirect is the best structural decision in the draft and it now carries the whole thesis in five short paragraphs: eight days glued inside a prosthetic suit with tears pooling in the mask, absorbed and then talked down ("It was eight days. I know that doesn't seem like a lot."), against "The thing she could not get through happened months earlier... It was a dance class." That inversion — she survives suffering and breaks at measurement — is the piece's novel insight, and it is argued rather than asserted.
- The revision pass closed the Evidence cap the right way. Replacing the flat weigh-in assertion with her own Evening Standard (June 2017) words — "a little roller that pinches you to measure the fat all over your body... every semester, you get a letter saying either you're too thin or you're okay or you're overweight... if you don't you're not asked back the next year" — makes the letter in the empathy turn literal instead of metaphorical. That single source upgrade is what turns "self-attack as armor" (house grammar) into "the self-attack is a way of arriving first" (hers).
- "The body parts Margaret Qualley can still list" earns its bespoke-form claim outright. An annotated inventory she volunteered unprompted — feet, hands, height, knees — each paired with the verdict that produced it, closing on the ballet school that made the grading official and then pivoting to "At twenty-eight she signed up for a five-month shoot whose entire subject is a woman being appraised." It collapses if transplanted to any other subject.
- Critic pressure is real, not decorative. Rooney/THR's "shrill, annoying flake" is an adverse read of her acting (not her scripts); the unfalsifiability objection to this analysis is conceded in its own paragraph ("If she boasted, that would be the Achiever. She deflects, and that becomes the Achiever too"); and Lanthimos's "doesn't take herself too seriously" is planted as the strongest outside evidence against the thesis before being answered. Most drafts duck one of these three; this one takes all three.
