## WATCH OUT (errors found + unverifiable claims)

- **CRITICAL DRAFT ERROR — "226.79 in the free skate" is WRONG.** 226.79 is Liu's **TOTAL combined** score in Milan. Her **free skate** was **150.20**. The draft states "She scored 226.79 in the free skate" (FAQ, line ~64) and "The score in the free skate was 226.79" (line ~277). Both attribute the combined total to the free skate. Must be fixed in any revision. (confirmed)
- **DRAFT CHRONOLOGY likely WRONG — "MacArthur Park… 2025–26 Olympic season / days before the Cup of China."** The Lady Gaga music-rights problem and the revert to MacArthur Park happened **days before Cup of China in November 2024**, which is the **2024–25 season**, not the 2025–26 Olympic season. MacArthur Park was a re-used program from the prior season; she then rode it to the 2025 Worlds title (March 2025). The draft frames this swap as happening "at the start of the 2025–26 Olympic season," which conflates two seasons. Recommend rewording. (confirmed timeline; draft framing disputed)
- **"24-year drought" is correct, but some outlets said "20-year."** Sarah Hughes won in 2002; 2026 − 2002 = 24 years. Olympics.com/NBC use "24 years." One SI headline used "20-year drought." Use **24**. (confirmed)
- **Free-skate placement nuance:** Liu was **3rd after the short program** and won the free skate (1st) to take gold — a come-from-behind win, not a wire-to-wire one. The draft's Milan section should reflect she trailed two Japanese skaters after the SP.
- **Could NOT independently re-verify** the exact "MacArthur Park Suite" wording at the **Olympics** free skate from a primary results page (program name widely reported for the 2024–25 program and 2025 Worlds; Milan free-skate program reuse is reported in profiles but not pulled from an official protocol here). Treat the "same program at Worlds and Olympics" claim as **likely-confirmed, not protocol-verified.**
