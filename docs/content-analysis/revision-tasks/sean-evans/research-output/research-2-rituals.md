# Research Brief 2 — Hot Ones Rituals

Show: _Hot Ones_, host Sean Evans, by First We Feast. Format: 10 wings, escalating Scoville, interview questions get harder as heat climbs.

**1. Da Bomb (Beyond Insanity) — wing 8**
Confirmed. "Da Bomb Beyond Insanity" sits in the **#8 slot** and is the universally dreaded one — not because of Scoville (only ~135,600 SHU, far below the finale) but because its pepper-extract base delivers a harsh, chemical heat that ambushes even spice veterans. Its reputation as the lineup's wall is a running in-joke; nearly every guest braces for the "Da Bomb face."

- https://chillychiles.com/blogs/news/da-bomb-beyond-insanity-explained-hot-ones-hot-sauce-guide
- https://www.saucemania.com.au/hot-sauce/a-complete-list-of-hot-ones-hot-sauces/

**2. The Last Dab — wing 10 ritual**
Confirmed. On the final wing, Evans invites the guest to add an _extra, voluntary_ dab of the hottest sauce — "It's tradition around here to put a little extra on the last wing — you don't have to if you don't want to." Most guests do it anyway, mid-suffering. First We Feast sells its own **"The Last Dab"** sauce line (built with Smokin' Ed Currie's Pepper X; later "Reduxx"/"Xperience" iterations), and the ritual is what the product is named after.

- https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hot_Ones
- https://heatonist.com/collections/hot-ones-hot-sauces-season-23

**3. "Wing of shame" — UNCONFIRMED / likely wrong term**
Could not verify "wing of shame" as an official Hot Ones term. The documented term is the **"Hall of Shame"** (guests who don't finish all 10 wings; renamed "Wall-Most Made It" from Season 17). Flag for writer: do not present "wing of shame" as a real ritual unless a specific clip can be sourced. If they mean the final wing + Evans's closing promo question ("what are we promoting?"), that is the Last Dab wing, not a "shame" wing.

- https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hot_Ones
- https://hot-ones.fandom.com/wiki/Hall_of_shame

**4. Explain That Gram**
Confirmed. A recurring mid-interview segment where Evans pulls up the guest's own Instagram posts and asks for the backstory. Became a regular beat in the show's later-2010s run (widely cited as **~2018, Season 6 era**) — treat the exact season as approximate unless verified.

- https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sean_Evans_(interviewer)
- https://www.facebook.com/HotOnesFWF/posts/364448610952530/

**Best support for the "Last Dab = counterphobic 6" angle:**
The voluntary extra dab — Evans explicitly says _you don't have to_ — and the guest does the harder thing anyway. A counterphobic Type 6 runs _toward_ the feared thing to master it; opting into more pain when given a clean exit is the cleanest, fan-checkable hook.
