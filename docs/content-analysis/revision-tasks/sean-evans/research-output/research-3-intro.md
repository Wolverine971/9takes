# Research Brief 3 — Hot Ones Intro Line (Verbatim)

**The exact current opening line (Season 30, 2026):**

> "Hey, what's going on everybody? For First We Feast, I'm Sean Evans and you're watching Hot Ones. It's the show with hot questions and even hotter wings. And today we're joined by [guest]..."

Verbatim from auto-captions of three current episodes (David Duchovny, Emily Blunt, PinkPantheress), word-for-word identical in all three. The line runs straight into a scripted guest bio, then "[Guest], welcome to the show."

**Citation:** Emily Blunt, Hot Ones — First We Feast/YouTube: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6rXotBDLLyE (also confirmed: David Duchovny https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7qgIJVm5Fo4 ; PinkPantheress https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lwbbtwE3XBY). Tagline corroborated by https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hot_Ones

**Variant note:** The tagline clause is sometimes paraphrased in press as "the show with hot questions, _and_ even hotter wings" (with a comma) — current spoken delivery has no pause: "hot questions and even hotter wings." Older-era references quote just the tagline fragment, but the full greeting formula above is the stable current form. No fixed scripted sign-off line exists; episodes close with the guest plugging projects, not a Sean catchphrase.

**Flag:** Quote the full greeting, not the standalone tagline, to make the "identical-every-time" Type 6 point land.
