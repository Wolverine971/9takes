# Research Brief 4 — Health Beat

**Genuine, on-record quotes (not jokes):**

1. **Off-camera diet + TUMS + tolerance (his clearest health statement).** Evans to People: _"When it comes to spicy food, because of the show, it makes me take care of myself a little bit more outside of that show. So a lot of salads, a lot of juice [and] a lot of cereal when I'm not doing it... So when I'm off the clock, keep the TUMS handy and try to avoid the super spicy stuff."_ Source: https://www.thethings.com/sean-evans-health-after-years-of-eating-spicy-wings-on-hot-ones/ (attributed to People Magazine)

2. **He knows the physical toll cold (tolerance, not immunity).** Evans to People: _"I know exactly, physiologically, what's going to happen."_ Same source.

3. **He won't touch wings off-clock.** Evans to Vanity Fair: _"Wings are dead to me. I do not eat wings off the clock."_ Source: https://www.thethings.com/are-sean-evans-spicy-wings-on-hot-ones-actually-real/

**Reported fact (not a quote):** Production caps shoots at ~3 episodes/week to give his stomach recovery days. Source: https://www.indiewire.com/features/craft/hot-ones-wings-production-interview-1235017469/

**Note on the "fainting" angle:** Evans fainted on set in 2020, but reporting indicates this was _not_ attributed to the wings — do not frame it as wing-caused.

**Ship a health beat: YES** — multiple genuine, citable Evans quotes (TUMS, salads/juice, "wings are dead to me," "exactly, physiologically, what's going to happen"). Frame it as managed tolerance and behind-the-scenes self-care, not damage or injury. Avoid implying lasting medical harm; no source supports that.
