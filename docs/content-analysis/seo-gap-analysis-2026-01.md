# 9takes SEO Gap Analysis & Competitive Strategy

**Date:** January 6, 2026
**Purpose:** Identify high-value keyword opportunities and competitive gaps to prioritize content creation

---

## Executive Summary

9takes has established strong authority in the **Enneagram + Mental Health** niche, ranking #1 for queries like "enneagram mental illness" and "enneagram type most prone to anxiety." However, significant gaps exist in relationship content, parenting, and emerging search behaviors around dating apps and therapy integration.

## Part 1: Competitive Landscape Analysis

### Tier 1 Competitors (High Authority)

#### 1. Truity (truity.com)

- **Strengths:**
  - World's most popular Enneagram test (10M+ completions)
  - Beautiful UX, no email signup required for free report
  - Established brand recognition
  - Cross-links to MBTI and other personality systems
- **Weaknesses:**
  - Content is surface-level ("listicle" style)
  - No psychological depth (missing the "why")
  - Lacks problem-focused framing
  - No childhood wound → adult pattern connections
- **9takes Advantage:** Deep psychological analysis, mental health integration

#### 2. Enneagram Institute (enneagraminstitute.com)

- **Strengths:**
  - Gold-standard RHETI test ($12, widely considered most accurate)
  - Original Riso-Hudson content
  - Academic credibility
- **Weaknesses:**
  - Dated website design
  - Premium content gated behind paywall
  - No modern SEO optimization
  - No problem-focused content
- **9takes Advantage:** Free, SEO-optimized, problem-focused framing

#### 3. Psychology Junkie (psychologyjunkie.com)

- **Strengths:**
  - Susan Storm is a certified MBTI practitioner
  - Emotional, engaging titles
  - Good cross-promotion with Truity
  - Active social presence
- **Weaknesses:**
  - Surface-level analysis
  - Missing root cause explanations
  - No actionable scripts or solutions
- **9takes Advantage:** Deeper analysis, practical frameworks, scripts

### Tier 2 Competitors (Medium Authority)

#### 4. EnneagramTest.com

- **Strengths:** Click-worthy titles, relationship focus
- **Weaknesses:** Thin content, no depth
- **9takes Advantage:** Comprehensive guides

#### 5. Brain Manager (brainmanager.io)

- **Strengths:** Celebrity type pages, clean design
- **Weaknesses:** Quantity over quality
- **9takes Advantage:** 284+ celebrity analyses (largest database)

#### 6. Enneagram Universe (enneagramuniverse.com)

- **Strengths:** Pop culture storytelling
- **Weaknesses:** Limited depth
- **9takes Advantage:** Psychological rigor

### Tier 3 Competitors (Forum/Community)

#### 7. Personality Cafe Forums

- **Threat Level:** HIGH for ADHD searches
- **Analysis:** Forum threads dominate "enneagram ADHD" searches, indicating low-quality official content in this space
- **Opportunity:** Create definitive ADHD + Enneagram guide to capture this traffic

## Part 4: Competitive Differentiation Framework

### 9takes Unique Value Proposition

| Competitor Standard | 9takes Standard                    |
| ------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Behaviors only      | Behaviors + **WHY** (psychology)   |
| Not addressed       | Childhood wound → adult pattern    |
| Generic advice      | Type-specific scripts & frameworks |
| Binary (good/bad)   | Healthy ↔ unhealthy spectrum      |
| Rarely connected    | Always mental health connected     |
| "Be aware"          | "Here's exactly what to say"       |

### Content Quality Checklist

Every new post should include:

- [ ] Problem-focused title (not description-focused)
- [ ] Quick Answer box at top (Featured Snippet optimization)
- [ ] Root cause explanation (childhood patterns)
- [ ] Healthy → unhealthy spectrum
- [ ] Actionable scripts/frameworks
- [ ] FAQ section with schema markup
- [ ] Comparison tables where relevant
- [ ] Internal links to related content

---

## Part 5: Technical SEO Recommendations

### Quick Wins

1. **Add Quick Answer boxes** to top 20 performing pages
2. **Implement FAQ schema** on all guide pages
3. **Add HowTo schema** to practical posts
4. **Reduce hedging language** ("may experience" → "commonly experience")
5. **Add comparison tables** to competitive keywords

### Content Structure Optimization

```markdown
# Clickbait Title (problem-focused)

<QuickAnswer>
Direct 2-3 sentence answer for AI/Featured Snippets
</QuickAnswer>

## Introduction (validate the problem)

## Main Content (organized by type or topic)

## Healthy vs Unhealthy Spectrum

## Practical Scripts/Frameworks

## FAQ Section (with schema)

## Related Reading (internal links)
```

### AI Search Optimization

For ChatGPT/Perplexity/Google AI Overview:

- Use definitive language (not hedging)
- Lead with direct answers
- Include numbered lists and tables
- Add clear type comparisons
- Maintain authoritative tone
