The pop culture section strategy focuses on **group dynamics analysis** - comparing clusters of people through the Enneagram lens rather than individual profiles. This creates:

- Higher viral potential (multiple fan bases)
- Rich comparison tables (SEO/featured snippet capture)
- Cross-linking to individual `/personality-analysis/` pages
- Pattern recognition that showcases 9takes' unique value

## Template Structure for Group Blogs

Every group dynamics blog should include:

1. **QuickAnswer** - Direct comparison summary
2. **Cast Table** - All members with their types
3. **Type Breakdowns** - Section per type represented
4. **Interaction Dynamics** - How they clash/cooperate
5. **Comparison Matrix** - Table format for AI/snippets
6. **Pattern Recognition** - What the group tells us about the industry/context
7. **FAQs** - Common questions
8. **Cross-links** - To individual personality analysis pages and type guides
