## The Strategic Framework

Every person wants 3 things. These map to the 3 intelligence centers of the Enneagram:

| Pillar          | Triad         | Core Drive        | What People Feel                                        |
| --------------- | ------------- | ----------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| See It Coming   | Head (5,6,7)  | Fear → Foresight  | "I didn't see that coming" / "I wish someone warned me" |
| Know What To Do | Gut (8,9,1)   | Anger → Agency    | "Just tell me what to do about it"                      |
| Feel Understood | Heart (2,3,4) | Shame → Belonging | "Am I the only one who feels this way?"                 |

**Primary verbs:**

- **Reveal** (Head) — see what you're missing
- **Equip** (Gut) — know what to do
- **Connect** (Heart) — feel understood

## Pillar 1: See It Coming

_Head Center — Fear Triad — Types 5, 6, 7_

### Headline

> You don't know what you don't know — and that's where the damage happens.

### Body

Most bad outcomes aren't caused by stupidity. They're caused by blind spots. You only see the world through your lens, which means 8 other emotional realities are invisible to you.

9takes surfaces the perspectives you're missing — before they blindside you.

### Feature Connections

- **Questions** reveal 9 different reads on the same situation. Your blind spot is someone else's obvious.
- **Personality analysis** decodes why people do what they do — so you can anticipate instead of react.
- **Guides** give you frameworks for navigating situations before they blow up.

### Tagline

> The pattern you're not seeing is the one that gets you.

## Pillar 2: Know What To Do

_Gut Center — Anger Triad — Types 8, 9, 1_

### Headline

> You don't need more theory. You need something that works.

### Body

Understanding personality types is useless if it stays abstract. You need to know: when your coworker shuts down, what do you _actually say_? When a relationship hits friction, what's the _move_?

9takes turns personality insight into practical action.

### Feature Connections

- **Questions** source field-tested approaches from real people — not therapists theorizing, but humans navigating the same mess you're in.
- **Personality analysis** shows how each type operates under pressure — concrete examples, not vague descriptions.
- **Guides** are step-by-step. Not "understand yourself better." More like "here's what to do tonight."

### Tagline

> Insight without action is entertainment. We build tools.

## Pillar 3: Feel Understood

_Heart Center — Shame Triad — Types 2, 3, 4_

### Headline

> The loneliest feeling: "Nobody gets why I see it this way."

### Body

You've had the experience — you react to something, and everyone around you acts like your reaction is wrong. Not just different. _Wrong._

9takes proves that every way of seeing the world has its own logic. Your reaction makes sense. So does theirs.

### Feature Connections

- **Questions** show you that your perspective isn't broken — it's one of 9 valid emotional realities.
- **Personality analysis** gives you the "that's me" moment. Seeing yourself in someone you recognize is how shame dissolves.
- **Guides** normalize the full range of human responses. There's no wrong type — just different foundations.

### Tagline

> You're not weird. You're a type. And your type makes sense.
