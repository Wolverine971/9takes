7. **"Opening the Kimono®"** — a $7,500–$10,000/seat, trademark-registered, 3-day conference in Napa (Aug 2011, ~130 people). Ran exactly once. _Confirmed, primary._ [tim.blog](https://tim.blog/2011/04/12/opening-the-kimono/) — Obscurity 4
8. **His right-hand man burned out and quit over dinner** — Charlie Hoehn hit debilitating anxiety running the 4-Hour Body launch, wrote _Play It Away_; Tim published the cautionary tale on his own blog. _Confirmed (2 primaries)._ [tim.blog](https://tim.blog/2014/02/13/anxiety-treatments/) · [charliehoehn.com](https://www.charliehoehn.com/2014/02/14/welcome-tim-ferriss-readers-2/) — Obscurity 4

- **Analytical note:** the timeline is the single strongest artifact for the Type 1 reading — every failure entry is self-prosecuted and converted into a rule within a sentence ("Such is the joy of baseless overconfidence," "It seems I still don't"), and the "Because I say so" authority script recurs three times across 18 years (kindergarten teacher, ice cream boss, TrueSAN sales director). The 2007 line "work as an excuse to be hyperactive and avoid the big questions" prefigures the 2026 OCD-rumination disclosure by 19 years.
