# Strategic Question Map: Which Question, Which Blog, Which Moment

Date: 2026-07-15
Method: two parallel agents read the top 20 content pages by GSC clicks (90-day window, 2026-07-06 export) in full. For each page: emotional thesis, the exact mirror-moment passage where the question belongs (25 to 60 percent depth band per the engagement evidence), demand-cluster mapping (from `2026-07-15-enneagram-demand-gap-research.md`), question candidates written to the T-12 design principles with a Type 1 vs Type 9 divergence test, and shared-question groupings.
Feeds: `docs/taskers/T-12-strategic-question-cta.md` Phase 0 (this document is that phase's deliverable).

## The headline

**DJ's conditional holds: the ADHD/neurodivergence set feeds the top-ranked question.** But the winning question is not an ADHD question. It is the masking question, which the ADHD reader, the depression reader, and the undiagnosed "no label fits" reader can all answer. That is the pattern across the whole map: the best questions sit one level beneath the page topic, at the feeling the topic is about.

## Global ranked shortlist

### #1: The Masking Question (recommended pilot)

> **"What's something you do every day to seem 'fine' that nobody knows is costing you effort?"**

- **Serves:** `enneagram-and-adhd-which-types-struggle-most` (252 clicks / 5,205 imps), `depression-patterns-by-enneagram-type` (85 / 4,994), `neurodiversity-vs-personality` (low GSC, exact thesis match), and `mental-health/enneagram-neurodivergence-guide` (141 / 4,977) IF DJ clears the safety flag (see structural findings).
- **Reach:** 337 clicks / 10.2k impressions on clean pages; 478 / 15.2k including the flagged guide.
- **Clusters:** `no-personality-just-coping` + `which-self-is-real`.
- **Divergence test:** Type 1 "Triple-checking everything before anyone sees it; exhausted by 10 AM." Type 9 "Nodding along in meetings; nobody notices I left my body an hour ago."
- **Placements:** ADHD page after the Type 4 flooding scene (line ~259, ~34% depth); depression page after the Type 7 hidden-depression passage (line ~327, ~52%); neurodiversity-vs-personality after the "you never asked to be sick, you asked to be explained" passage (lines ~108-114, ~25%).

### #2: The Partner-Quiet Question (natural wave 2)

> **"Your partner's been quiet all evening. What story does your head start telling, and what do you actually do?"**

- **Serves:** `attachment-styles-and-enneagram-types` (42 / 7,517, host: directly after the Maya vignette, line ~213, ~29%), `how-each-enneagram-type-manipulates` (34 / 2,778, Type 5 withholding section, line ~306), `toxic-traits-relationships-warning-signs` (44 / 2,797), `enneagram-compatibility-matrix` (81 / 10,735, after T-05 lands).
- **Reach:** 120 clicks / 13.1k imps now; 201 / 23.8k post-T-05.
- **Cluster:** `husband-shuts-down`, the highest-recurrence cluster in the demand research, in its purest form.
- **Divergence test:** Type 1 "I audit: did I do something wrong? Then I ask directly, because ambiguity is worse than any answer." Type 9 "I match the quiet. If it's about me, I'd rather not find out tonight."

### #3: The Praise-Cost Question (cleanest, most reusable)

> **"What do people praise you for that secretly costs you the most?"**

- **Serves:** `enneagram-strengths-and-weaknesses` (22 / 1,800, host), `biggest-compliments-to-give-each-enneagram-type` (31 / 1,017), `enneagram-types-and-career-choices` (23 / 3,224), `toxic-traits-of-each-enneagram-type` (54 / 3,233).
- **Reach:** 130 clicks / 9.3k imps, all pages clean, zero flags.
- **Clusters:** three at once: `helpful-and-resentful`, `people-pleaser`, `achievement-is-the-wound`.
- **Divergence test:** Type 1 "Being reliable. It means I'm the one staying late fixing what other people half-did, forever." Type 9 "Being easygoing. Nobody asks what I want anymore. Including me."

### #4: The Attraction-Flip Question (honorable mention)

> **"What trait first attracted you to someone that later became the thing you couldn't stand?"**

- Serves `toxic-traits-relationships-warning-signs` (host, existing PopCard slot at line ~201), `attachment-styles`, `toxic-traits-of-each`, and the compatibility matrix post-T-05 (it is that page's thesis). 140 / 13.5k now, 221 / 24.3k with the matrix. Cluster: `keep-attracting-unavailable`. Everyone has an answer in ten seconds; the answers are a personality x-ray. Held at #4 only because its biggest page is behind the T-05 flag and #2 already covers this page group.

### #5: The Oversized-Reaction Question (alternate for the pilot set, do not run alongside #1)

> **"Someone makes an offhand comment about something you did, the dishes, an email, a wrong turn. Walk us through the next ten minutes inside your head."**

- Serves the same ADHD/neurodivergence set as #1 plus `enneagram-types-in-stress` and `enneagram-mental-health-flags` (~428 / 13.8k). Clusters: `oversized-reaction` + `adhd-remainder`. The richest divergence scaffold in the map (the "ten minutes" forces a scene). One question per page: this is the A/B alternate if #1 underperforms, not a co-tenant.

## Structural findings (surfaced by the research, need owners)

1. **Dual-URL bug on the whole `mental-health/` subdirectory.** `src/routes/enneagram-corner/[slug]/+page.server.ts` globs `src/blog/enneagram/**/*` recursively, so every `mental-health/` file resolves at BOTH `/enneagram-corner/<slug>` and `/enneagram-corner/mental-health/<slug>`. GSC indexes both and splits equity (visible on the addiction guide: 18 clicks at each URL). Needs a canonical or redirect fix BEFORE measuring any pilot on those pages. Not owned by any tasker yet.
2. **Safety-flagged hosts.** `enneagram-neurodivergence-guide` and `enneagram-addiction-recovery-guide` both carry quality D / safety-gate FAIL / rebuild priority in frontmatter. Embedding a community give-first feature on safety-flagged mental-health pages is a sequencing decision only DJ can make. Recommendation: pilot #1 on the three clean pages first; add the guide after its rebuild.
3. **The corpus already knows it wants this mechanic.** Several pages end with their own per-type reflection questions at 95 to 99 percent depth (`toxic-traits-of-each` line ~581, `careers` lines ~397-409), where median readers never arrive. The instinct existed; the placement was wrong.
4. **Three pages have existing component slots (PopCard/Marquee) within a few lines of the recommended insertion point**, making them the cheapest implementations: toxic-traits-relationships (~l.201), toxic-traits-of-each (~l.296), strengths-and-weaknesses (~l.293).
5. **Reference-shaped pages discount linear placement.** Compatibility matrix, wings, subtypes are anchor-jump pages; a single mid-band slot misses jump readers. Subtypes is an honest null; skip it.

## Recommended sequence

1. **Wave 1 (the pilot):** Question #1 (masking) on `enneagram-and-adhd`, `depression-patterns`, `neurodiversity-vs-personality`. One question, three pages, ~337 clicks of monthly reach, zero flags.
2. **Wave 2:** Question #2 (partner-quiet) on `attachment-styles` + `how-each-type-manipulates` + `toxic-traits-relationships`, once wave 1 validates the mechanic. Add the compatibility matrix after T-05.
3. **Wave 3:** Question #3 (praise-cost) on its four clean pages; add the neurodivergence guide to wave 1's question after its rebuild; revisit frozen `enneagram-and-mental-illness` only with DJ's explicit sign-off.
