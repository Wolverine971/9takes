## David Rockefeller, Epstein & the Trilateral Commission

### Who Was David Rockefeller?

David Rockefeller (1915–2017), grandson of Standard Oil founder John D. Rockefeller, was a central figure in 20th-century American finance and international relations:

([CIA](https://www.cia.gov/resources/csi/studies-in-intelligence/studies-in-intelligence-vol-69-no-3-extracts-september-2025/banker-philantropist-soldier-spy-david-rockefellers-experience-in-army-intelligence-1942-54/))

- **1960:** Became President of Chase Manhattan Bank

- **Connections to Allen Dulles:** Knew CIA Director Allen Dulles and Secretary of State John Foster Dulles since college. Allen Dulles set up his WWII operations center in Rockefeller Center. John Foster Dulles was a trustee of the Rockefeller Foundation (1935–1952).

([Wikipedia: David Rockefeller](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/David_Rockefeller); [Britannica](https://www.britannica.com/money/David-Rockefeller))
