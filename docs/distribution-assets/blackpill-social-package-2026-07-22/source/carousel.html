9takesThe social / women loop05 / 10

Loop one

The social loop manufactures the evidence it expects.

  1. 01“Women already hate me.”
  2. 02Avoid talking—or enter defensive.
  3. 03The interaction feels tense.
  4. 04She keeps her distance.
  5. 05More rejection and isolation.
  6. ↺“See? Women hate me.”

Avoidance alone can keep ordinary, neutral counter-evidence from entering the picture.

Women are people—not a category, judge, or prize.
9takesThe economic / class loop06 / 10

Loop two

The money loop is not “just mindset.”

  1. 01“I will always be poor.”
  2. 02Little stability or money.
  3. 03Less mobility, training, and access.
  4. 04Fewer contacts and openings.
  5. 05Exhaustion and stalled momentum.
  6. ↺“See? There is no way up.”

The barriers are real. The permanent sentence is the added claim.

Skills · referrals · training · transportation · support can reveal another door

Build a ladder with other people on it.
9takesIf you are trying to help07 / 10

Start with what is true

Validate the pain. Do not validate the sentence.

Validate the pain

“You have been lonely.”

“The job search has been brutal.”

“That rejection hurt.”

Do not affirm

“Your future is fixed.”

“Every woman is the enemy.”

“Nothing can change.”

“What part is a fact—and what part predicts the rest of your life?”

Compassion and disagreement can coexist.
