## Reddit Post — r/Enneagram

**Title:** Celebrity Type 5 Case Study: Shawn Ryan — The SEAL Who Built a Podcast Empire by Listening

**Body:**

I've been studying Enneagram patterns in public figures and Shawn Ryan is one of the most compelling Type 5 cases I've come across.

Quick context: Shawn Ryan is a former Navy SEAL (Teams 2 and 8, 20+ deployments), CIA Global Response Staff contractor, and host of the Shawn Ryan Show (currently #2 on Spotify behind Joe Rogan).

Here's what makes the Type 5 reading interesting:

**The listening style.** His podcast dominates not because he's charismatic or controversial, but because he asks questions and gets out of the way. His longest interview was nine hours. He learned this from three years of twice-weekly therapy — his therapist barely spoke, and he realized silence draws people out better than interrogation.

**Self-described introversion.** Despite running a top-10 global podcast, he says: "I'm not a very public person, I don't like going out in public... I sure as hell don't do public speeches." His happiest place is an off-grid property at 10,500 feet in Colorado with solar power and a fox that visits the porch.

**Intelligence as a literal career.** SEAL reconnaissance. CIA surveillance and pattern analysis. He chose careers built on observation and operating in the shadows.

**Even his addiction was systematic.** During his dark years in Colombia, he mapped drug supply chains from street level up — finding dealers, then their dealers, then the source. The investigator framework doesn't turn off.

**Emotional distance.** His most revealing quote: "I've hid my emotions for so long I don't even know how to access them anymore." That's the 5 pattern of retreating so far into the observer role that you lose access to your own inner life.

**Where it breaks down:** His raw advocacy for veterans ("Why can't you just let us get better?"), his screaming at strangers during a Sedona meltdown, and his craving for more combat intensity all push against the pure 5 reading. Healthy complexity.
