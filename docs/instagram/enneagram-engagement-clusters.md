# Enneagram Engagement Clusters — Where Each Type Lives Online

**Purpose:** A fishing map for @9takesdotcom engagement. People don't post _"I'm an Enneagram 2."_ They post _"why do I abandon myself in every relationship."_ Each type has a cluster of pop-psychology topics, vernacular self-ID terms, and creator communities already talking about its core wound — without ever naming the Enneagram. This doc maps that vernacular → type, so warmup can find, follow, and engage the right rooms and then quietly route them to 9takes.

**How to use:** Feeds `/instagram-warmup` account sourcing and `/instagram-reply`. For each type: the **self-ID vernacular** (what they actually say / search), the **hashtags**, the **creator/community clusters** to follow, the **mega-trend** it rides, and the **engagement angle** (how 9takes adds value without being the "well actually, that's Enneagram" guy).

**Core principle:** Lead with their language, not ours. Validate the pattern first; the Enneagram is the deeper map we offer _after_ we've been useful. Never diagnose a stranger. Never reply "you're a Type X." We name the _pattern_, not the person.

**Adopted:** 2026-06-17 · Owner: DJ Wayne

## Type-by-Type Map

### Type 1 — The Perfectionist (anger turned inward)

- **Self-ID vernacular:** "recovering perfectionist," "my inner critic is brutal," "Type A," "I have impossibly high standards," "perfectionist procrastination," "I can't relax / I feel guilty resting," "all-or-nothing," "I'm too hard on myself," "eldest daughter," "good girl conditioning," "I notice every flaw," "secretly so angry."
- **Hashtags:** #perfectionism #perfectionist #recoveringperfectionist #innercritic #eldestdaughter #eldestdaughtersyndrome #typeApersonality #highachiever #procrastination #goodgirlsyndrome #parentification #burnout
- **Creator/community clusters:** productivity & self-improvement, "clean girl"/organized-life aesthetic, eldest-daughter content, ADHD-procrastination coaches, nervous-system "you're allowed to rest" accounts, anti-burnout, Stoicism/discipline pages (the disciplined-male slice).
- **Rides:** the perfectionism + eldest-daughter waves.
- **Engagement angle:** name the inner critic as a _separate voice_, not a fact. "The standard moved the second you hit it, didn't it?" Offer relief, not more optimization.
- **Anchor figure:** Tim Ferriss.

### Type 2 — The Helper (pride; needs hidden behind giving)

- **Self-ID vernacular:** "people-pleaser," "I have no boundaries," "I abandon myself," "the strong friend / therapist friend," "I give too much and get taken for granted," "empath," "I attract narcissists," "codependent," "fawn response," "I lose myself in relationships," "mom friend," "savior complex," "martyr." _(Dark extreme: Munchausen / caretaking-as-control — sensitive, do not invoke casually.)_
- **Hashtags:** #peoplepleaser #peoplepleasing #codependency #boundaries #fawning #emotionallabor #therapistfriend #strongfriend #empath #selfabandonment #narcissisticabuse #anxiousattachment
- **Creator/community clusters:** boundary coaches, codependency-recovery, **narcissistic-abuse-survivor / "empath" accounts** (huge), anxious-attachment recovery, caregiver-burnout, "stop abandoning yourself" self-love, fawn-response trauma.
- **Rides:** the boundaries movement + the narcissist/empath dyad.
- **Engagement angle:** validate the cost of over-giving and the buried need. "What do _you_ need right now — and when did you last ask?" The narcissist-survivor crowd is the richest sub-pool; meet them as the over-giver, not the victim.
- **Anchor figure:** _no published **celebrity** analysis yet (candidates: Dolly Parton, Princess Diana–coded givers, Mister Rogers). Bridge still works — route to the **type page** `/personality-analysis/type/2` or the **type blog** `enneagram-corner/enneagram-type-2` ("why you can't say no"), which mirrors this pond directly._

### Type 3 — The Achiever (image; worth tied to winning)

- **Self-ID vernacular:** "imposter syndrome," "I tied my worth to productivity," "high-functioning," "I don't know who I am without achieving," "chameleon," "my online life is fake," "I performed happiness," "workaholic," "golden child," "burnt out from succeeding," "two-faced" (others' word for them).
- **Hashtags:** #impostersyndrome #hustleculture #burnout #highfunctioning #thatgirl #workaholic #goldenchild #productivity #personalbrand #validation
- **Creator/community clusters:** ambition-burnout, imposter-syndrome content, hustle-culture critique, "high-functioning anxiety," authenticity coaches, founder/entrepreneur mental health, "that girl"/optimization aesthetic, golden-child family-roles.
- **Rides:** imposter syndrome + hustle-burnout.
- **Engagement angle:** separate the win from the worth. "Who are you when nothing's being measured?" Respect the drive while naming its bill.
- **Anchor figures:** Sabrina Carpenter, Charli XCX.

### Type 4 — The Individualist (envy; identity & longing)

- **Self-ID vernacular:** "main character energy," "I romanticize my sadness," "I feel everything too deeply," "I never fit in," "too much / not enough," "highly sensitive person," "yearning," "I make my pain into art," "no one really gets me," "I want what everyone else has."
- **Hashtags:** #sadgirl #maincharacterenergy #romanticizeyourlife #hsp #highlysensitiveperson #feelingtoomuch #yearning #melancholy #emoadult #toomuch
- **Creator/community clusters:** poetry & spoken-word, sad-girl aesthetic, romanticize-your-life, HSP communities, art-as-therapy, journaling/shadow-work, astrology (water signs), "feel your feelings."
- **Rides:** the main-character / sad-girl aesthetic.
- **Engagement angle:** honor the depth without feeding the specialness-through-suffering loop. "The feeling is real _and_ you're not broken for having it."
- **Anchor figure:** Lana Del Rey.

### Type 5 — The Investigator (avarice; withdraw to conserve)

- **Self-ID vernacular:** "I live in my head," "I need so much alone time," "social battery at 0," "overthinker," "I observe instead of participate," "hyper-independent," "I intellectualize my feelings," "INTJ/INTP," "deep thinker," "I don't need anyone," "hyperfixation."
- **Hashtags:** #introvert #overthinking #avoidantattachment #hyperindependence #intj #intp #deepthinker #socialbattery #hyperfixation #intellectual
- **Creator/community clusters:** **MBTI INTJ/INTP** content (heavy overlap), avoidant-attachment recovery, introvert advocacy, philosophy/rationalist, minimalism, neurodivergent "systemizer" accounts, "deep thinker" pages.
- **Rides:** introvert + avoidant-attachment + MBTI INTJ.
- **Engagement angle:** respect the need to understand; gently name that watching isn't the same as living. "You've mapped it. What would it cost to be _in_ it?"
- **Anchor figure:** Robert Greene.

### Type 6 — The Loyalist (fear; scanning for safety)

- **Self-ID vernacular:** "anxiety," "overthinker," "catastrophizing," "waiting for the other shoe to drop," "trust issues," "I need reassurance," "what-ifs," "anxious attachment," "I question everything," "decision paralysis," "fight-or-flight," "nervous system is fried."
- **Hashtags:** #anxiety #anxious #overthinking #catastrophizing #anxiousattachment #nervoussystem #fightorflight #trustissues #whatifs #worstcasescenario
- **Creator/community clusters:** **anxiety-management (the biggest pool of all)**, somatic/nervous-system-regulation, anxious-attachment, health-anxiety, "the anxious overthinker," found-family, skeptic/question-authority (counterphobic).
- **Rides:** anxiety — the single largest mental-health conversation online.
- **Engagement angle:** the warmest, safest, highest-volume pond. Validate the vigilance as loyalty/care, not weakness. "Your brain is trying to keep everyone safe — it just never clocks out."
- **Anchor figure:** Pedro Pascal.

### Type 7 — The Enthusiast (gluttony; outrun the pain)

- **Self-ID vernacular:** "FOMO," "I can't sit still," "ADHD brain," "too many hobbies," "I say yes to everything," "shiny object syndrome," "I numb with fun," "dopamine chasing," "I avoid hard feelings," "10 tabs open," "commitment-phobe."
- **Hashtags:** #adhd #adhdbrain #fomo #dopamine #restless #toomanyhobbies #shinyobjectsyndrome #multipassionate #burnout #noveltyseeking
- **Creator/community clusters:** **ADHD creators (massive)**, dopamine-menu/-detox, "multi-passionate," anti-toxic-positivity, travel/adventure, sobriety/recovery (numbing-via-substance), entrepreneur-with-100-ideas.
- **Rides:** ADHD + dopamine culture.
- **Engagement angle:** fun first, then the quiet question. "What's the feeling the next thing is helping you skip?" Don't kill the joy; name the avoidance under it.
- **Anchor figure:** Kai Cenat.

### Type 8 — The Challenger (lust/intensity; control as protection)

- **Self-ID vernacular:** "control issues," "I'm too intense," "people find me intimidating," "I have walls up," "soft inside, armor outside," "I don't back down," "strong independent woman," "I push people away," "I protect everyone but no one protects me," "trust is earned."
- **Hashtags:** #boundaries #controlissues #intense #strongwoman #protector #toughlove #wallsup #vulnerability #notbackingdown #divinemasculine
- **Creator/community clusters:** fierce-boundaries content, trauma-and-control, protector/"divine masculine," activism/justice intensity, anger-work, "intimidating woman reclaiming it," underdog-defender pages.
- **Rides:** the assertive-boundaries + "too much woman" reclaiming lane.
- **Engagement angle:** honor the armor and name what it guards. "The walls kept you safe. Who gets to be on the inside?"
- **Anchor figure:** Chappell Roan.

### Type 9 — The Peacemaker (sloth; self-forgetting)

- **Self-ID vernacular:** "conflict-avoidant," "I don't know what I want," "I disappear in relationships," "I go along with everything," "I check out / numb," "passive-aggressive," "I can't get started," "I keep the peace at my own expense," "I abandon myself to avoid a fight," "stuck."
- **Hashtags:** #conflictavoidant #peoplepleaser #selfabandonment #numbing #procrastination #findingyourvoice #passiveaggressive #freezeresponse #stuck
- **Creator/community clusters:** conflict-avoidance, "finding your voice," self-abandonment recovery (overlaps 2), freeze-response/dissociation trauma, "what do I actually want," procrastination/inertia, mediators.
- **Rides:** the freeze-response + self-abandonment slice of the boundaries movement.
- **Engagement angle:** hardest to find (9s post least about themselves). Best back-door is the freeze-response and "I lost myself keeping the peace" content. "Your preference matters even when no one's asking — _especially_ then."
- **Anchor figure:** _no published **celebrity** analysis yet (candidates: Keanu Reeves, Mister Rogers (2/9 debate), Barack Obama–coded calm). Bridge still works — route to the **type page** `/personality-analysis/type/9` or the **type blog** `enneagram-corner/enneagram-type-9` ("why you disappear to keep everyone comfortable"), which mirrors this pond directly._

## Engagement Playbook (how to actually work the rooms)

1. **Start with the three giant ponds** on a still-warming account: **Anxiety (6)**, **People-pleasing/Narcissist-survivor (2)**, **ADHD (7)**. Biggest volume, lowest drama-risk, most universal.
2. **Find rooms via:** the hashtags above; the comment sections of large therapy/psych creators; trending audios used by "POV: you're the [pattern]" content; attachment-style and MBTI accounts (back-doors into multiple types).
3. **Follow** 5–10 accounts per priority cluster; **engage before posting at them** — comment value first (validate the pattern, add one insight), days before any link.
4. **Comment formula:** validate their words → add one specific pattern insight → leave the door open. No "that's Enneagram Type X." No diagnosing. (See [feedback: Instagram comment style] — short, varied tone, no AI tells.)
5. **Route to 9takes only after warmth:** the bridge is whatever destination best mirrors the stranger's type — the celebrity person-analysis when one is published, otherwise the **type page** (`/personality-analysis/type/N`) or the **type blog** (`enneagram-corner/enneagram-type-N`). A stranger in the "people-pleaser" room meets our Type 2 content, not a cold "take the test" pitch. Every type has a live destination; no pond is unbridgeable.
6. **Match content to pond:** when posting into a cluster's week, lead the caption with _their_ vernacular hook (the type-first caption format already does this — see `personality-series-north-star.md` and the intro-arc lineup).
