## Engagement Rules by Tier

### Tier 1

- Check every scan
- Comment on posts <24h old
- Reply to Stories when relevant
- Build genuine relationships (DM when appropriate)
- Visibility Level: 1 or 2 (these audiences know Enneagram)

### Tier 2

- Check every scan
- Focus on posts where Enneagram knowledge adds invisible depth
- Visibility Level: 0 (never mention Enneagram directly)
- Be the person in comments who "just gets it"

### Tier 3

- Check 2-3x per week
- Focus on MBTI bridge moments (add depth MBTI can't)
- Celebrity posts where 9takes has published analysis
- Visibility Level: 0-1

### Tier 4

- Engage when discovered during scan
- Evaluate after 2-3 engagements whether to promote to higher tier
- Log observations for weekly review
