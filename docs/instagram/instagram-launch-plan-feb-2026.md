## Part 1: Series Introduction

### What Is This Content Block?

A recurring Instagram series analyzing the personality patterns behind public figures using the Enneagram. Not gossip. Not hot takes. The real psychology underneath.

### Series Name Options

| Name                    | Vibe                                           | Pros                                                   | Cons                                  |
| ----------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------- |
| **The Pattern**         | Clean, intellectual, pattern-recognition brand | On-brand, memorable, implies you see what others don't | Slightly abstract for cold audience   |
| **What's Driving Them** | Action-oriented, curiosity-inducing            | Clear value prop, warm language                        | Longer, less punchy as a series title |
| **The Read**            | Short, confident, "this is my read" voice      | Conversational, implies insight not judgment           | Could be confused with book content   |
| **9 Ways to See It**    | Directly from the 9takes tagline               | Ties back to platform                                  | Too brand-centric for cold audience   |
