## Sabrina Carpenter

- Her parents nicknamed her "bumblebee" as a reminder to stay humble before she hit double digits.

- At Coachella 2024 she wore a "Jesus was a carpenter" T-shirt to absorb the church-video scandal.

## Anne Hathaway

- For Les Misérables, Anne ate "two thin squares of dried oatmeal paste a day" during a two-week filming break and lost 25 pounds.
- Her mother Kate played Fantine in the first U.S. tour of Les Misérables — the same role Anne later won an Oscar for.
- Anne considered becoming a nun at age 11 after feeling "a calling from God."
- The entire Hathaway family converted from Catholicism to Episcopalianism after her older brother Michael came out as gay.
- She quit drinking entirely in 2018 after dropping her son off at school while hungover — "That was enough for me."
- During Mother Mary, choreographer Dani Vitale spent two years (sometimes 8 hours a day) trying to "break" Anne out of her precision — and the dance training revealed her real singing voice is alto, not the soprano she'd performed in her whole life.
- On her ninth wedding anniversary, Anne ditched the romantic spa for a sauerkraut-making workshop while husband Adam took a nap.
- During Les Misérables filming in London, she had to ask Adam to leave the set because his presence made her too happy to play Fantine's despair.
- She and Meryl Streep confronted Devil Wears Prada 2 producers about skeletal models after observing Milan Fashion Week, securing concrete promises about their film's models.
- A heel broke during filming in New York and she tumbled down steps on camera — she got up immediately because the crew's hearts had "jumped up into their throats."

## Alex Cooper

- At age five, Alex sat in the back of her father's control room wearing a miniature headset while he produced Philadelphia Flyers broadcasts.
- By eighth grade she'd logged roughly 10,000 hours editing in Adobe Premiere in her family basement.
- Catholic school classmates slammed her head into the ground until she lost consciousness — she kept it secret from her parents.
- "Call Her Daddy" downloads jumped from 12,000 to 2 million per episode within two months of launching from her New York apartment in 2018.
- She lost roughly 5,000 Instagram followers overnight after interviewing Kamala Harris in October 2024 — and called it "totally worth it."
- She turned down an $8 million brand deal because she "didn't believe in putting my face next to it."
- Her hiring philosophy: "If you're not trying to take my job, then I don't want you at the company."
- She walked herself down the aisle at her April 2024 Riviera Maya wedding to Matt Kaplan; the entire wedding party ended the night swimming in the ocean in formal attire.
- After her SiriusXM deal, she bought her parents a New England-style house in Los Angeles about 15 minutes from her own.
- Her Unwell brand became the official partner of the National Women's Soccer League via a $3 million sponsorship — full circle for the D1 player whose coach refused to say her name.
