## Why it's built this way

- **Niche keyword in first 3 words** — "Personality analysis is what I do" — matches the discoverability lever flagged in `saved-posts-analysis-2026-05-18.md` (keyword in first 5 words of caption).
- **Extends, doesn't repeat** — the slides cover "what I do + I'm starting a series + there's always more underneath." The caption extends with the _concrete_ version of "more underneath": the intimidating/chill/nice triplet. That's the actual hook for why the series is worth following.
- **Triplet is the showpiece line** — "intimidating → scared / chill → exhausted / nice → furious" is specific enough to make a reader tag someone, which is the engagement signal IG rewards.
- **CTA models the existing series voice** — "Drop a name. I'll go." mirrors the closer from the 7-slide launch carousel for continuity across both intro variants.
- **Voice check** — tactically direct, pattern-recognition focused, mild contrarian sharpness only where the target (zodiac / surface-typing content) earns it. No therapy-talk softeners.

### Hashtag swap options

If the post performs and you want to rotate hashtags on subsequent series posts to avoid stale clustering:

- Replace `#peoplereading` → `#humanbehavior` or `#psychology`
- Replace `#celebritypsychology` → `#famouspeople` or `#publicfigures`
- Replace `#selfawareness` → `#characteranalysis` or `#mbti` (broader reach, less precise)

Keep `#enneagram`, `#personalityanalysis`, `#9takes` as the anchor set.
