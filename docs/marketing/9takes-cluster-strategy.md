# 9takes Famous People Cluster Analysis

## Fan Account Outreach Strategy

For each cluster, identify:

1. **Reddit communities** (r/KUWTK, r/YouTubers, r/popheads, etc.)
2. **Twitter/X fan accounts** (dedicated stans)
3. **Instagram fan pages** (meme accounts, update accounts)
4. **TikTok commentary accounts** (drama channels, analysis)
5. **Discord servers** (fan communities)

**Outreach Template:**
"Hey! I run 9takes.com — we do Enneagram personality analysis. Just published a deep dive on [Cluster Name] and their types. Thought your community might find the [Specific Person] analysis interesting. Here's the link: [URL]"

---

## Cross-Linking Strategy

In each individual blog, add:

- "Related: [Other Person in Cluster]'s Type Analysis"
- "Read the full [Cluster Name] Group Analysis"
- Sidebar: "Other [Type] Celebrities"

This creates the rabbit hole effect you're looking for.
