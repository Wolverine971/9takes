## Follow-Up Rules

1. **Wait 5–7 days** before following up
2. **One follow-up max** — if they don't respond to 2 messages, move on
3. **Follow-up should add value** — share a new analysis you just published, or reference something they recently posted
4. **Never pitch in a public comment** — always DM or email
