## Question content pack (for /questions)

What belief have you changed in the last 3 years, and what changed it?
What’s one issue where you disagree with “your side”?
What emotion sits under your strongest political opinion?
What would make you update your view on [topic]?
When did someone make you feel understood while disagreeing with you?
What do people misunderstand most about your position?
Which argument against your view is strongest?
What’s a belief you hold mostly because your community holds it?
What topic makes you feel “if I’m wrong, who am I?”
What do you wish opponents asked you before judging you?
