The Enneagram frame (Type 4 meaning-making vs Type 6 fear-scanning) is genuinely different and valuable, but needs to be positioned as an alternative lens, not a correction. Save this angle for a future session after building more credibility on the platform.

- **Type 9 is overrepresented in this queue** (3 questions) — good news: the Type 9 angle is extremely underserved on Quora. No one is explaining stonewalling through the Type 9 lens of "going numb to preserve peace." Strong differentiation potential.

- **Type 7 is completely absent** — for next session, search specifically for questions about impulsivity, commitment-phobia, FOMO, or "why do some people avoid boredom at all costs."

- **Overthinking questions** (100+ answers) were too crowded to queue this session but are worth a targeted search. Look for a more specific version: "why do I overthink even when I know it's irrational" — likely fewer answers and cleaner gap.

- **"Passive-aggressive" is a revisit** — the Type 1 / Type 9 angle there is clean and the gap is real. But at 98 answers, wait until 2-3 answers are posted to build ranking before entering crowded questions.

- **Blog matches are strong this session** — 5 of 6 queued questions have a specific, relevant 9takes post to link. Ghosting question is the gold standard: `enneagram-types-being-ghosted.md` is a direct match.
