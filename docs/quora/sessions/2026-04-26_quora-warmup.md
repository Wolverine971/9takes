The Quora algorithm punishes early/spammy linking and personality-blog linking specifically gets flagged if the answer reads as ad copy.
- **Author-as-Type-4 risk:** Five of eight queued threads are Type 4 framings. If posting in one session, space them out — same voice on Strauss + Rubin within an hour can read as "this guy types everyone as 4."
- **Horowitz weakness:** Could not find a clean "what's Ben Horowitz like personally" thread. The strongest Horowitz threads are book-summary or tactical-advice questions where a personality angle reads as derail. Worth doing a manual scan of the `/topic/Ben-Horowitz` page later for fresher questions, or wait until a Horowitz news cycle prompts a new question.
