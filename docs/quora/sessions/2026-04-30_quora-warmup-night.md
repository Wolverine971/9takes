- **Type 4 melancholy-as-identity gap closed** — entry that was held back from 2026-04-29 PM session due to overload. The "feels good to be sad" top answer is the cleanest possible Type 4 setup on Quora.
- **Type 3 gap remains open after this session** — ran 6+ Type 3 search variants (validation, success-money, image, mask, accountability, chameleon, attention-seeking, focused-on-success). Every clean candidate either: (a) was already covered by prior queue, (b) had only ~6 visible answers, (c) was 200+ answers and saturated, or (d) used first-person "how do I stop" framing. Type 3 needs a different search shape next session — try "what is the psychology behind status anxiety", "why do successful people feel like frauds" (Type 3 imposter angle), or "why do some people perform their personality."

Quora doesn't seem to use observer-mode terminology. Try clinical-shape next session: "why do some people analyze emotions instead of feel them" or "why do some people retreat into their thoughts."
- **Multi-type contrast questions remain top-leverage** — three of five queued (#3 ambition built-in contrast, #4 procrastinate four-engine, #5 stuck-in-life multi-type) follow the "different types do this for different reasons" shape that scored 90+ in prior sessions.
