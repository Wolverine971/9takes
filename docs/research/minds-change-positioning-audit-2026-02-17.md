## What 9takes Already Has (Strong)

### Product-level differentiation already aligned with research

- Give-first mechanic reduces social priming and mimetic copying.
- Question-first architecture encourages reflection over performance.
- Anonymous + personality context creates identity-safe but meaningful discussion.

### Content-level assets already in place

- anti-mimetic and anti-echo-chamber arguments
- tactical disagreement content (steelmanning, curiosity)
- emotional literacy and listening playbooks
- Enneagram-based empathy translation layer

## Gaps Exposed by the Two-Book Synthesis

1. No flagship page that names the full model end-to-end.
2. The model is implied, but not explicitly framed as:
   - self-updating
   - social/identity safety
   - system design
3. Limited reusable scripts for real conflict moments.
4. No recurring "belief-shift stories" format to prove outcomes.
5. Internal docs understate depolarization as a first-class positioning lane.

## Recommended Positioning Upgrade

### Core claim

9takes is not only a personality site.

9takes is a **depolarization-by-design conversation system** that helps people:

- think independently before social influence
- understand emotional drivers behind disagreement
- engage conflict with lower identity threat

### 3-Layer message framework

- **Layer 1 (Self):** Rethinking mindset (`Think Again` fit)
- **Layer 2 (Social):** Identity-safe dialogue (`How Minds Change` fit)
- **Layer 3 (System):** Give-first conversation architecture (9takes differentiator)
