Date: 2026-07-06
Window tested: 2026-04-05 to 2026-07-04
Primary data: fresh Google Search Console export in `docs/data/gsc/2026-07-06-*`
Secondary data: Google autocomplete probes, repo inventory, current corpus stats

## Executive Read

9takes should not chase generic Enneagram head terms first. The best opportunity is still the intersection of:

1. Enneagram plus public figures
2. Enneagram plus mental health/neurodivergence
3. Enneagram compatibility and relationship intent
4. First-party corpus statistics
5. Pop-culture cast/personality maps

The highest ROI work right now is not a net-new keyword brainstorm. It is packaging existing visibility better. The site is already getting page-one or near-page-one impressions for celebrity personality queries, compatibility queries, ADHD/autism queries, subtype/blind-spot queries, and corpus-statistics queries. CTR is the constraint.

## Bigger Opportunities

### Compatibility Tool Cluster

GSC and autocomplete both say the compatibility matrix should become more than a long article.

Build:

- Existing page: optimize for `enneagram compatibility chart`.
- New lightweight tool: `/enneagram-corner/enneagram-compatibility-calculator`.
- New query pages only after testing: `2 and 7 compatibility`, `2 and 5 compatibility`, `2 and 4 compatibility`.

External SERP check showed general lifestyle/health publishers ranking for compatibility content, including Brides and Verywell Mind. This means the space is competitive, but not exclusively owned by Enneagram-native authorities.

### Mental Health / Neurodivergence Cluster

This is the best current content moat. Low GSC impressions, very high CTR:

- `enneagram and ADHD`: 62 impressions, 20.97% CTR, position 5.2.
- `adhd enneagram`: 99 impressions, 7.07% CTR, position 4.5.
- `enneagram and autism`: 98 impressions, 12.24% CTR, position 5.7.
- `enneagram mental illness`: 38 impressions, 34.21% CTR, position 3.0.

Expand carefully with health disclaimers:

- `enneagram and anxiety`
- `enneagram and trauma`
- `enneagram and narcissism`
- `which enneagram is most likely to be depressed`
- `which enneagram is most likely to be a narcissist`

### Test Comparison Cluster

The page is visible but not winning clicks:

- `best free enneagram test`: 389 impressions, position 14.5.
- `cloverleaf enneagram test`: 309 impressions, position 9.0.
- `best enneagram test free`: 281 impressions, position 12.7.

Optimize before creating new test content. Searchers want a table with free/paid, time to complete, email required, accuracy caveats, and best use case.
