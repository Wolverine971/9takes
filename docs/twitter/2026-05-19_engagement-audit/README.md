# Twitter Engagement Audit — @9takesdotcom — 2026-05-19

## What this is

A deep, live-scan audit of @9takesdotcom's actual X presence and the engagement ecosystem around it. Performed via browser automation: scanned the profile, both home feeds (Following + For You), four search queries (#enneagram top + latest, "enneagram" broad, "personality type", "mbti"), two adjacent-community searches (attachment style, psychology/therapist), and a sample of the existing following list.

## Headline findings

- **30 followers / 179 following after 5 years.** Average post: 6–33 views, 0 likes.
- **The enneagram community is dead on X.** Your own 2024 posts rank as the "Top" results for #enneagram.
- **The psychology + attachment-style community is hugely active and adjacent.** Dr. Nicole LePera, Heidi Priebe, Adam Lane Smith, Brian Maierhofer get 50K–1M views with content structurally identical to yours.
- **MBTI gets 800K–1M views per top post** by hijacking anime + K-pop fandoms. Enneagram has no equivalent vector.
- **You're following the wrong people.** Your follows are celebrity creators + political/finance noise. Zero of the mid-tier psychology accounts that would actually engage.
- **You post like an SEO writer, not a Twitter user.** Link cards kill your reach. The brand voice underperforms your human "felt cute, might delete later" voice.

## The strategic move

Stop trying to build "Enneagram Twitter." It doesn't exist on this platform — the enneagram community lives on Instagram, YouTube, Substack, TikTok. Instead: **be the enneagram voice inside the active Psychology + Attachment Twitter community.** Hijack the formats that already work there ("According to psychology…", "How to deal with X type", type-as-identity one-liners, "personality types of [TV show / K-pop idol]") and translate enneagram content into them.
