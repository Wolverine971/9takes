## Tweet 2 — Framework

The Enneagram maps 3 intelligence centers:

→ Head (Types 5, 6, 7) — driven by fear → needs foresight
→ Gut (Types 8, 9, 1) — driven by anger → needs agency
→ Heart (Types 2, 3, 4) — driven by shame → needs belonging

Every person leads with one. But all 3 matter to everyone.
