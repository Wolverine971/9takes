## 2. The world in one paragraph

> **The 9takes world is a confessional booth that opens into a chorus.** One situation sits in the middle of the room. You give your take before you see the crowd, because the cost of entry is honesty. Then the curtain lifts: eight other people looked at the same moment and felt something you could not have generated on your own. The magic system is the give-first reveal. The hero is the person tired of misreading people, tired of being misread, and ready to see their own emotional logic beside the other eight. The villain is the internet that collapses human complexity into one winning take: the early Reddit comment, the crowd-approved answer, the personality label, the shallow quiz result. The founder is not a guru handing down verdicts; he is an Enneagram 8 who had to learn that his wife's fear was not a malfunction. 9takes is where people stop diagnosing each other and start seeing the emotions behind every take.

**Working rule:** the founder story should hand the mirror to the reader. The world is not "DJ understands the Enneagram." The world is "you have an alarm system too, and you keep mistaking other people's alarms for defects."

## 3. Element-by-element mapping

### 01 - Named Concepts

The portable ideas people should carry away from 9takes.

**Recommended canon, tiered:**

- ✅ **The banner: "See the emotions behind every take."** This is already the primary positioning tagline. It names the whole world: opinions are surface; emotional logic is underneath.
- ✅ **The atomic object: "The Nine."** One situation, nine coherent emotional responses, one slot per Enneagram type. This is the first-class object in `docs/product/the-chorus-vision.md`.
- ✅ **The reveal: "The Chorus."** The emotional payoff after a user writes first. It should mean "the moment you physically feel that your way of seeing is one of nine."
- ✅ **The ritual: "Give-first unlock" / "Reveal through response."** The user writes before seeing. This is the sacred threshold. Recommendation: use **give-first unlock** in product UI because it is plain; use **Reveal through response** in strategy/docs because it names the mechanism.
- ✅ **The doorway: "The Mirror Moment."** The social/content version of the same move: the subject's pattern becomes a question about the reader's pattern, landing on a 9takes question page.
- 🔶 **The growth lane: "Personality-maxing."** Strong for social/dating/tactical psychology audiences. Risk: it can sound too self-optimization-coded if it becomes the whole brand. Recommendation: keep it as a content lane, not the master banner.
- 🔶 **The place: "Streetlamp Symposium."** Already defined in brand/design docs as the visual identity. It needs a worldbuilding translation: not just colors, but the recurring place where anonymous truths can be said clearly.

**Bench, but useful in content:** emotional foundation mapping, open source conflict resolution, mental gym for your EQ, ancient wisdom modern application, give your take first, questions over verdicts, self-discovery over diagnosis.

**Naming hierarchy to ratify:**

1. Banner: See the emotions behind every take.
2. Object: The Nine.
3. Ritual: Give-first unlock.
4. Payoff: The Chorus.
5. Content doorway: The Mirror Moment.

### 02 - Re-defined Terms

Vague words 9takes should make actionable.

**Recommended four to repeat forever:**

1. ✅ **"Take" = an emotional read of reality, not just an opinion.** A take tells you what someone noticed, what alarm fired, and what they think the situation demands.
2. ✅ **"Type" = a recurring alarm pattern, not a verdict.** A type is a center of gravity for fear, shame, or anger. It is not a box and not something 9takes shoves down someone's throat.
3. ✅ **"Conflict" = channel mismatch before fact mismatch.** Many fights are not about the stated topic first; they are about whose alarm system gets treated as real.
4. ✅ **"Anonymous" = protected honesty, not consequence-free cruelty.** The point is to make vulnerable answers possible before crowd pressure distorts them.

**Bench:** personality test = doorway, not destination; Reddit = crowd gravity, not true Q&A; comment = a data point in the chorus, not a performance for upvotes; self-discovery = a door opened from the inside.

### 03 - Weaknesses

The relatable flaws and comeback patterns.

**Public-safe canon:**

- ✅ **The founder blind spot:** DJ is an Enneagram 8. He understood anger from the inside and treated fear like a malfunction until marriage forced a better map. This is the cleanest origin weakness because it directly creates the product.
- ✅ **The typing-evangelist trap:** DJ used to tell people their type before they were ready. 9takes refuses that posture. People do not want to be labeled; they want to recognize themselves.
- ✅ **The complexity trap:** the Enneagram can get buried under wings, instincts, arrows, and origin debates. 9takes should lead with anger, fear, shame; three intelligences; nine patterns. Depth can wait until the user wants it.
- ✅ **The empathy test:** the famous-person analyses started as growth, but became empathy practice. If the analysis makes even disliked people more human, the method is working.
- 🔶 **The product weakness:** the empty-chorus problem. A give-first reveal that opens to sparse answers breaks the promise. This is not just UX debt; it is worldbuilding debt.
- 🔶 **The proof gap:** there is no canonical stat yet showing that give-first answers are meaningfully different from post-crowd answers. That would be a flagship receipt.

**Internal-only guardrails from founder docs and Sensitive Index:**

- Do not publish family Enneagram types.
- Do not use DJ's dad's death as public content/clout.
- Do not push unresolved family conflict or sensitive employment/failure mechanics.
- The Enneagram "psyop" joke remains needs-ruling.

**User comeback pattern:** "I thought they were being difficult. Then I saw the alarm I do not have." Every archetype map should translate that into its own situation.

### 04 - Characters & Settings

Recurring people, voices, rooms, and scenes.

**Characters:**

- ✅ **DJ** - founder, Enneagram 8, the person who had to learn fear was real.
- ✅ **Anna** - the 7 whose fear DJ had to stop dismissing; origin catalyst, not a mascot.
- ✅ **The question asker** - the person carrying a real situation and needing uncrowded perspective.
- ✅ **The nine voices** - not stereotypes, but nine emotional logics placed side by side.
- ✅ **The famous person under the lens** - the public example that teaches the reader how motivation shapes a life.
- ✅ **The reader's own pattern** - surfaced by the Mirror Moment.
- 🔶 **The AI understudy** - holds the chorus open until humans arrive. Must be clearly labeled and retired when human takes out-resonate it.
- 🔶 **The skeptic** - "isn't this astrology?" Important recurring character because the Enneagram's credibility must stay defensible.
- 🔶 **The self-appointed typer** - antagonist cameo; the person who turns a framework into a verdict.

**Settings:**

- ✅ **The cool-down after the fight** - where someone finally asks, "why did we keep doing that?"
- ✅ **The question page before unlock** - a quiet room, not a feed.
- ✅ **The Chorus reveal** - the curtain lift; your take seated beside eight alternatives.
- ✅ **The empty frame** - Mirror Moment image grammar: the subject's space emptied and turned toward the reader.
- ✅ **The Streetlamp Symposium** - warm stone, amber light, anonymous but not faceless; the visual home for hard honesty.
- 🔶 **The Reddit thread** - the negative setting: early comments snowball, late depth dies, lurkers stay quiet.

**Visual/moodboard need:** define the reusable visual set for The Nine, the locked curtain, the chorus reveal, the empty frame, and the Streetlamp Symposium. The brand colors exist; the recurring world objects do not.

### 05 - Interests & Values

What 9takes shares with its audience.

**Values:**

- ✅ Understanding over judgment.
- ✅ Self-discovery over diagnosis.
- ✅ Questions over verdicts.
- ✅ Authentic perspective before crowd influence.
- ✅ Practical application over theory.
- ✅ Ancient wisdom with receipts.
- ✅ No ads and no selling user data, because the data is psychologically personal.
- ✅ Curiosity as a skill, not a vibe.

**Interests that can become connection surface:**

- Enneagram, motivation, and social dynamics.
- Public-figure lore and mini-biography as empathy engine.
- Tactical self-improvement and "read the room" skill-building.
- Debates, conflict, argument repair, and persuasion.
- The internet's social architecture: Reddit, lurkers, comments, upvotes, groupthink.
- Founder-side intensity: wrestling, Marine Corps, startup building. Use as color, not the brand headline.

**Keystone founder identity:** DJ's Enneagram 8 identity is native to 9takes, not a trivia detail. It explains the origin mistake, the product refusal to diagnose people, and the obsession with directness.

### 06 - Lore & Legends

Backstory that gives the teaching credibility.

**Canon lore beats:**

1. ✅ **The marriage origin:** newlywed conflict; "take a personality test"; MBTI opens the door; Enneagram goes deeper.
2. ✅ **The 8/7 emotional translation:** anger made sense to DJ; fear did not. This becomes the first proof that different alarm systems are real.
3. ✅ **The DC church small group:** the first experience of hearing different types process the same material together.
4. ✅ **The personality Facebook dead end:** the first product idea was rejected because labeling people felt dystopian.
5. ✅ **The forum/type-police scar:** arguing with someone who has already decided what you are is miserable.
6. ✅ **The Reddit design insight:** Reddit is close, but early-comment power laws and lurker architecture prevent authentic answer-first Q&A.
7. ✅ **The Enneagram scraper:** DJ scraped Enneagram discourse because the obsession was already real; that side project later helped him get the Curri job.
8. ✅ **The empathy engine:** writing famous-person analyses made people DJ disliked more understandable.
9. 🔶 **The always-complete Nine:** if shipped well, this becomes product lore: AI seeds hold the chorus open until real human takes replace them.

**Lineage to cite carefully:** Plato/triadic soul framing, modern psychology, ego development research, Ian Cron as personal entry point, Big Five as the scientific contrast. Keep this defensible; do not overclaim the Enneagram as established science.

### 07 - Common Enemy

People rally against one apex villain, not a heap of complaints.

🔶 **Recommended apex villain: "the machine that collapses nine takes into one."**

**Head 1 - Crowd gravity:** Reddit/upvote/feed architecture that rewards speed, agreement, performance, and early anchoring. It turns a question into a winning comment.

**Head 2 - The label machine:** shallow quizzes, type police, PersonalityDB-style voting, and typology debates that replace recognition with diagnosis.

**Henchmen:** Buzzfeedification of personality, "just communicate better" advice, academic psychology with no daily-life bridge, wing/subtype overcomplication before the basics, gossip without empathy, AI/personality slop, empty-chorus reveals, and the urge to type someone before they are ready.

**Guardrails:**

- Punch systems, not people.
- Do not make Reddit users, Enneagram communities, or skeptical people the enemy.
- Enemy framing should resolve into relief: 9takes gives you the room the internet usually cannot.
- Keep the product's own failure modes in the frame. If the chorus is empty or the mirror feels like diagnosis, 9takes is behaving like the enemy.

## 5. What we need to do for 9takes

### Immediate documentation work

1. **Ratify the naming hierarchy.** Without this, the world has too many cool phrases and no "Force" concept.
2. **Create a one-page public world bible.** A short public-safe page that defines The Nine, give-first unlock, The Chorus, and the enemy without internal notes.
3. **Update brand docs after ratification.** `docs/brand/brand-positioning.md`, `docs/brand/brand-style-guide-v2.md`, and the BlogPurpose/About copy should inherit the same names.

### Product flow work

1. **Make the question flow feel like the world.** Situation -> give-first unlock -> mirror/read of user's answer -> Chorus reveal -> next question.
2. **Solve the empty-chorus problem.** Always-complete Nine, AI-seeded and clearly labeled, with human promotion/replacement.
3. **Turn personality-analysis traffic into question-page traffic.** Top analyses should end with a Mirror Moment that lands on the exact question page, not a generic related-posts grid.
4. **Build the private thread of self later.** It is powerful, but downstream of proving the first reveal.

### Content and distribution work

1. **Use the three archetype maps.** Conflict poster, personality-analysis reader, and social navigator each need their own hooks, settings, and comeback pattern.
2. **Ship the content queue.** Start with the enemy and ritual posts: "The internet buries the nine takes under one," "Give first or get groupthink," and "Your strongest channel feels like common sense."
3. **Create a visual/moodboard pass.** Define images for The Nine, locked curtain, Chorus reveal, empty frame, Streetlamp Symposium, and the Reddit anti-setting.
4. **Build Reddit participation around the world, not promotion.** The enemy is not Reddit users; the wedge is that Reddit's architecture makes certain conversations hard.

### Measurement work

Track whether the world is becoming real:

- write-before-see rate
- reveal completion rate
- second-question rate
- return rate after first reveal
- AI seed replacement rate by humans
- number of complete human Nines
- Mirror Moment click-through from social/personality pages
- user language adoption: are people saying "my take," "the nine takes," "the chorus," or only "comments"?
