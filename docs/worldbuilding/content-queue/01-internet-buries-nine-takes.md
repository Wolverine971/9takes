# The internet buries the nine takes under one

**Status:** queued
**Lane:** Brand / enemy
**Source:** [9TAKES_WORLDBUILDING_MAP.md](../9TAKES_WORLDBUILDING_MAP.md), Common Enemy.

## Hook

Most comment sections do not reveal what people think. They reveal which take got there first.

## Core idea

Reddit, feeds, and comment sections collapse a situation into one winning read. 9takes exists to hold the other eight reads open long enough for people to feel them.

## Product tie-in

The give-first unlock prevents anchoring. The Chorus reveal shows nine emotional logics side by side.

## Draft angle

Start with a familiar Reddit thread: early comment gets traction, later nuance dies. Then name what got lost: not "more opinions," but fundamentally different alarm systems.

## Dependency

Ratify the apex villain wording in the main map.
