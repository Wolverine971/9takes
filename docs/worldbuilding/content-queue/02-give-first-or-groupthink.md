# Give first or get groupthink

**Status:** queued
**Lane:** Product ritual
**Source:** `docs/product/the-chorus-vision.md`, `docs/brand/brand-positioning.md`.

## Hook

You cannot get someone's honest take after they have already seen the room.

## Core idea

The give-first mechanic is not friction for its own sake. It is the ritual that protects the reveal. The user writes before seeing because authentic perspective comes before influence.

## Product tie-in

Use this to explain the locked state on question pages and personality-analysis CTAs.

## Draft angle

Frame it as a respect move: 9takes asks for your take first because your unanchored read is valuable.

## Dependency

Decide whether UI copy should say "give-first unlock" everywhere or use a softer phrase on public pages.
