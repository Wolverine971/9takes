# Your strongest channel feels like common sense

**Status:** queued
**Lane:** Founder / philosophy
**Source:** `docs/brand/9takes-manifesto.md`, `docs/brand/founder-story.md`.

## Hook

Your strongest emotional channel feels like reality. Everyone else's feels like a bug.

## Core idea

Head, heart, and gut each have their own alarm: fear, shame, and anger. Most conflict starts when one person treats their alarm as real and the other person's alarm as irrational.

## Product tie-in

This is the clean educational bridge into The Nine: the nine types are recurring ways people rank and protect those channels.

## Draft angle

Use the public-safe founder example: DJ understood anger and had to learn to make space for fear.
