# The Mirror Moment

**Status:** queued
**Lane:** Social / product doorway
**Source:** `docs/product/the-mirror-moment.md`.

## Hook

The best personality content does not end on the subject. It turns the lens back on you.

## Core idea

Every story carousel and strong personality analysis should end with a question that the reader answers in their head, then lands on a 9takes question page.

## Product tie-in

Explains the social-to-question funnel and why bio links should point to question pages, not just blogs.

## Draft angle

Show the pattern: subject lore -> type reveal -> empty frame -> reader question.

## Dependency

Needs 3-5 live examples from upcoming carousels or top personality pages.
