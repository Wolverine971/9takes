# Enneagram 1 Audience Archetypes

Three audience maps for applying the Type 1 world to 9takes content and product surfaces. These archetypes describe the reader's job, not their Enneagram type.

## 1. The Self-Prosecuting Recognizer

**Job:** Decide whether the Type 1 lens explains a lifelong pattern - the critic, the standards, the exhaustion - without being told their conscientiousness is a disease.

**Arrives thinking:**

> People call me critical or uptight. Nobody sees that the voice I use on them is the gentle version of the one I use on myself.

**Needs:**

- recognition that the critic sounds like reason from inside, not like an attacker
- the critic/conscience distinction before any growth advice
- growth framed as dropping the trial, never as lowering the bar
- explicit permission to keep their standards and their seriousness
- experiments that preserve agency (deliberate imperfection as choice, not command)

**Resists:**

- "just relax," "let it go," "stop being so hard on yourself" as instructions
- being told their childhood caused this
- perfectionism pathologized wholesale, standards included
- celebration of sloppiness as the enlightened alternative
- any hint that self-compassion means becoming careless

**Best doorway:** The Inner Courtroom and the critic-vs-conscience test.

**Best question:** "What mistake are you still serving a sentence for?"

**Best CTA:** A private self-audit or give-first question before any social sharing.

**Success signal:** They can name the critic as a separate voice from their values, and schedule one recess without a receipt.

## 2. The Person Across From The One

**Job:** Understand a partner, parent, boss, or friend whose correction feels like rejection, without either excusing the harm or writing the person off as contemptuous.

**Arrives thinking:**

> I know they love me. So why do I feel graded all the time?

**Needs:**

- the reframe that correction can be the One's dialect of care, held together with the truth that the impact still counts
- the compression model: why the anger comes out as politeness, sighs, and sudden cold
- warning signs of the ledger (silent overwork, mounting formality) before the collection event
- concrete language: appreciation before advice, naming the atmosphere of evaluation
- permission to set boundaries on criticism without taking responsibility for the One's critic

**Resists:**

- "they only criticize because they care" as a full stop
- advice to preemptively perfect themselves to keep the peace
- being recruited as the One's defendant or their therapist
- type talk that excuses chronic hostile criticism

**Best doorway:** The Compression and the Resentment Ledger.

**Best question:** "When does their standard feel like shelter, and when does it feel like a scorecard?"

**Best CTA:** Compare takes on the same situation, then choose one repair request ("appreciation before advice").

**Success signal:** They leave with empathy for the trial the One lives inside, plus one sentence that protects their own footing.

## 3. The Type 1 Analyst

**Job:** Write or evaluate a personality analysis without mistaking discipline, activism, tidiness, or harshness for Type 1 motivation.

**Arrives thinking:**

> This person is exacting and principled. Is that a One, or just a professional with standards - or a 3 chasing excellence for the win, or a 6 checking for what could go wrong?

**Needs:**

- motivation evidence across contexts: correctness pursued when no one is watching and no reward follows
- direct quotes about being right, being good, self-criticism, resentment, and rule-keeping
- countertyping against 3 (excellence for image), 5 (precision for understanding), 6 (checking for safety), 8 (force for autonomy), and the SX1/8 confusion specifically
- the subtype spread, so an outwardly fiery reformer is not auto-typed 8 and a worried self-improver is not auto-typed 6
- separation of fact, interpretation, and Enneagram doctrine

**Resists:**

- typing from tidiness, politics, religiosity, or job title
- treating activism or moral language alone as proof of One-ness
- inventing a strict-childhood backstory to make the profile satisfying
- certainty about private motivation

**Best doorway:** The Gap as a hypothesis to test - does the subject's attention go to error even off duty, at cost, without audience?

**Best question:** "What repeated choice shows that being right mattered more than being liked, winning, or staying safe?"

**Best CTA:** Open the evidence ledger, inspect counterarguments, then invite reader takes.

**Success signal:** The analysis remains persuasive after every speculative sentence is labeled as interpretation.

## Surface Map

| Surface                    | Primary archetype           | Required move                                               |
| -------------------------- | --------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------- |
| Type 1 pillar page         | Self-Prosecuting Recognizer | Recognition -> critic/conscience split -> chosen experiment |
| Dating / relationship post | Person Across From The One  | Dialect-of-care empathy plus a concrete boundary            |
| Personality analysis       | Type 1 Analyst              | Evidence, motivation, countertype                           |
| 9takes question page       | All three                   | Give first, preserve agency, compare perspectives           |
| Social carousel            | Recognizer + person across  | Name the concept without turning it into a verdict          |
