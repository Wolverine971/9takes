## Bottom Line

The Type 1 world is not simply "high standards." It is a moral-survival map.

When the pattern is active, everything arrives with a gap between is and ought, and the gap feels personally assigned. The Inner Courtroom prosecutes the One before the world can, because somewhere the equation got written: a mistake is not something you made, it is evidence of what you are. The anger this generates is forbidden, so it compresses into resentment, criticism, and a very polite stiffness - and the compressed anger keeps books.

The 9takes Type 1 promise:

> You do not have to stop being good. You have to stop proving you are not bad.

The growth frame that keeps the type's gift intact: the harm was never in the standards, it was in the trial. Keep the bar. Drop the trial. Take the recess.
