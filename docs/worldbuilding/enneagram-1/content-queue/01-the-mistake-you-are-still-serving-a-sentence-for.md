# The Mistake You're Still Serving A Sentence For

**Status:** queued
**Surface:** question page / carousel / Mirror Moment
**Source:** [ENNEAGRAM_1_WORLDVIEW_BIBLE.md](../ENNEAGRAM_1_WORLDVIEW_BIBLE.md)

## Hook

What mistake are you still serving a sentence for?

## Core idea

The Inner Courtroom never adjourns on its own. Most people have one case that stayed open for years: the thing they said, shipped, broke, or failed to do, long since forgotten by everyone else, still being prosecuted nightly. The sentence is served in overwork, in deferred rest, in the compliment that cannot land.

## Structure

1. Everyone else forgot. The court didn't.
2. The critic sounds like reason, so the case sounds legitimate.
3. The conscience produces a next action; the critic produces a verdict.
4. If there's a repair, make it. That closes cases.
5. If there's no repair left, the sentence is just a habit. Adjourn.
