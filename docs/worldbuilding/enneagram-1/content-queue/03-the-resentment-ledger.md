# The Resentment Ledger

**Status:** queued
**Surface:** relationship post / dating content
**Source:** [ENNEAGRAM_1_WORLDVIEW_BIBLE.md](../ENNEAGRAM_1_WORLDVIEW_BIBLE.md)

## Hook

You did more than was asked. You told no one. You billed for it anyway.

## Core idea

Compressed anger keeps books. The One does invisible, unrequested, load-bearing work, and every unthanked correction accrues interest until it gets collected: the cold comment, the disproportionate explosion, the martyred "I'll just do it myself." Nobody agreed to the debts, so the collection lands as ambush.

## Structure

1. The invisible work (the whole point of doing it right is nobody sees a problem).
2. The ledger entries nobody signed.
3. The collection event.
4. The structural unfairness on both sides.
5. The repair: say the price before you pay it.

## Product tie-in

Question page: "What's in your resentment ledger, and who never knew they were being billed?" Also the anchor for partner-facing Type 1 content (appreciation before advice).
