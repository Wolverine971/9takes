# The Recess

**Status:** queued
**Surface:** growth explainer / carousel / coaching prompt
**Source:** [ENNEAGRAM_1_WORLDVIEW_BIBLE.md](../ENNEAGRAM_1_WORLDVIEW_BIBLE.md)

## Hook

In court, a recess pauses the trial. In childhood, recess is the part of the day nobody grades. The Type 1 growth line is both at once.

## Core idea

The 1-to-7 movement named in courtroom language: a scheduled adjournment that does not dismiss the case, fire the judge, or lower the bar. Play that does not have to be earned. The standards survive the day off; the bar was never load-bearing on the suffering.

## Structure

1. Why "just relax" bounces off Ones (it sounds like corruption).
2. Recess vs. dismissal: the case can stay important without staying in session.
3. What a recess looks like in practice (rest before the list is done, no receipt).
4. The evidence: the standards are still there Monday. Nothing degraded.
5. Schedule it. Adjournment you don't have to earn.
