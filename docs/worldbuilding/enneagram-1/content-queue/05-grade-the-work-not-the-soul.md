# Grade The Work, Not The Soul

**Status:** queued
**Surface:** work / leadership / craft
**Source:** [ENNEAGRAM_1_WORLDVIEW_BIBLE.md](../ENNEAGRAM_1_WORLDVIEW_BIBLE.md)

## Hook

There's a version of high standards that is joy, and a version that is a sentence. Same bar. Different defendant.

## Core idea

The craft distinction: watch a One in their workshop - patient repair, well-kept tools, the tenth revision that finally sings. Same standards, no trial, because the work is being graded and the worker is not. The perfectionism research agrees from the outside: harm tracks self-criticism (concerns), not high standards (strivings).

## Structure

1. Two Ones, same bar: the workshop and the courtroom.
2. The research split: strivings vs. concerns (analogy, cited honestly).
3. The leadership version: feedback that grades the work, reports that don't feel graded as people.
4. Keep the bar. Drop the trial.
