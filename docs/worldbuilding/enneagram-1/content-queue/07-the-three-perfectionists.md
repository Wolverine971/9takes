# The Three Perfectionists

**Status:** queued
**Surface:** subtype explainer / blog
**Source:** [ENNEAGRAM_1_WORLDVIEW_BIBLE.md](../ENNEAGRAM_1_WORLDVIEW_BIBLE.md)

## Hook

One of the three Type 1s is openly angry, aims the red pen at the world instead of themselves, and gets mistyped as an 8. "Perfectionist" is hiding three different people.

## Core idea

The subtype spread: SP1 (Worry - the true perfectionist, anger most repressed, self-improving), SO1 (Non-adaptability - the exemplar, anger cooled into certainty, teaches the right way by embodying it), SX1 (Zeal - the countertype, anger hot and outward, reforms others and society). Explains why many Ones reject the label on first contact.

## Structure

1. The stereotype (tidy, restrained, self-critical) fits only one of the three.
2. SP1 Worry. 3. SO1 Non-adaptability. 4. SX1 Zeal, the countertype.
3. Countertype guardrail: not every One looks tidy; some look like fire aimed at the world's flaws.
