# Enneagram 2 Audience Archetypes

Three audience maps for applying the Type 2 world to 9takes content and product surfaces. These archetypes describe the reader's job, not their Enneagram type.

## 1. The Depleted Recognizer

**Job:** Decide whether the Type 2 lens explains a lifelong pattern - the giving, the exhaustion, the blank where their own needs should be - without being told their warmth is a disorder.

**Arrives thinking:**

> People call me the strong one, the mom friend, the one who has it together. Nobody sees that I'm running on empty, and I don't know how to say it without feeling like a burden.

**Needs:**

- recognition that the blindness to their own needs is real, not laziness or false modesty
- the radar/ledger distinction before any growth advice: their warmth is a gift, the invoice is the problem
- growth framed as lighting their own side of the glass, never as caring less
- explicit permission to keep their generosity and their identity as a warm person
- experiments that preserve agency (one direct ask, one received gift) rather than commands ("just say no")
- the reassurance that finding their needs takes work; the blank is not proof there's nothing there

**Resists:**

- "just say no," "put yourself first," "you teach people how to treat you" as instructions
- being told their childhood caused this
- their giving pathologized wholesale, warmth included
- "people-pleaser" used as a diagnosis instead of a description
- any suggestion that boundaries mean becoming cold

**Best doorway:** The One-Way Mirror and the dissertation-vs-blank test.

**Best question:** "Who takes care of you?"

**Best CTA:** A private needs inventory or give-first question before any social sharing.

**Success signal:** They can name one need out loud, and receive one piece of care without repaying it inside the hour.

## 2. The Person Across From The Two

**Job:** Understand a partner, parent, friend, or coworker whose generosity sometimes feels like an invoice, without either dismissing the real warmth or absorbing the guilt.

**Arrives thinking:**

> I know they love me. So why do I feel indebted all the time? And why did they explode last month about things I never knew were wrong?

**Needs:**

- the reframe that the giving usually carries a coded request to be valued, held together with the truth that the guilt still lands
- the hint-economy model: why the Two never asked directly, and why the missed hints got logged as evidence
- warning signs of the ledger (mounting sighs, martyred cheerfulness, sudden coldness) before the collection event
- concrete language: asking twice past "I'm fine," naming the invisible work, insisting gently on giving back
- permission to decline unrequested help without accepting a guilt bill for it

**Resists:**

- "they're just being nice, be grateful" as a full stop
- advice to preemptively manage the Two's feelings forever
- being recruited as the Two's mind-reader or their debtor
- type talk that excuses chronic guilt-tripping

**Best doorway:** The Invisible Ledger and the Hint Economy.

**Best question:** "What has this person been carrying for you that you never actually asked for?"

**Best CTA:** Compare takes on the same situation, then choose one repair move ("ask twice," or "receive their care and turn the spotlight around").

**Success signal:** They leave with empathy for the fear under the giving, plus one sentence that protects their own footing.

## 3. The Type 2 Analyst

**Job:** Write or evaluate a personality analysis without mistaking warmth, a helping profession, or public generosity for Type 2 motivation.

**Arrives thinking:**

> This person is famously generous and beloved. Is that a Two, or a 9 keeping the peace, a 3 performing likability, a 6 building alliances, or just a kind person?

**Needs:**

- motivation evidence across contexts: care given when no one is watching, and distress specifically at being unneeded or unappreciated
- direct quotes about being needed, self-neglect, difficulty receiving, pride in indispensability
- countertyping against 9 (merging to avoid conflict), 3 (image of likability for success), 6 (loyalty for security), 7-social and SP2 specifically (the charming countertype who attracts care instead of giving it)
- the subtype spread, so an ambitious community-builder is not auto-typed 3 and a charming care-attractor is not auto-typed 7
- separation of fact, interpretation, and Enneagram doctrine

**Resists:**

- typing from profession, gender, or public generosity alone
- treating philanthropy or niceness as proof of Two-ness
- inventing an emotionally starved childhood to make the profile satisfying
- certainty about private motivation

**Best doorway:** The Radar as a hypothesis to test - does the subject's attention go to others' needs even off duty, at cost, without audience, and does being unneeded register as threat?

**Best question:** "What repeated choice shows that being needed mattered more than being right, winning, or staying comfortable?"

**Best CTA:** Open the evidence ledger, inspect counterarguments, then invite reader takes.

**Success signal:** The analysis remains persuasive after every speculative sentence is labeled as interpretation.

## Surface Map

| Surface                    | Primary archetype          | Required move                                              |
| -------------------------- | -------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| Type 2 pillar page         | Depleted Recognizer        | Recognition -> radar/ledger split -> one chosen experiment |
| Dating / relationship post | Person Across From The Two | Coded-request empathy plus a concrete boundary             |
| Personality analysis       | Type 2 Analyst             | Evidence, motivation, countertype                          |
| 9takes question page       | All three                  | Give first, preserve agency, compare perspectives          |
| Social carousel            | Recognizer + person across | Name the concept without turning warmth into a diagnosis   |
