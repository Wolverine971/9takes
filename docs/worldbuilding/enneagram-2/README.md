## Bottom Line

The Type 2 world is not simply "likes helping people." It is a love-survival map.

When the pattern is active, every room arrives as a set of needs in high definition, and meeting them feels like the price of being kept. The Two watches life through a one-way mirror: everyone else lit, their own side dark, their own needs locked in a room they can barely access on purpose. The giving is real warmth, and it also keeps books - the Invisible Ledger of unrequested, unthanked, unbilled care that accrues interest until collection day, when the sweetest person in the room turns suddenly ferocious and then drowns in shame about it.

The 9takes Type 2 promise:

> You do not have to stop giving. You have to stop using it to pay for love.

The growth frame that keeps the type's gift intact: a one-way mirror is just glass with the lights off on one side. Keep the warmth. Drop the ledger. Light your side, and the mirror becomes a window.
