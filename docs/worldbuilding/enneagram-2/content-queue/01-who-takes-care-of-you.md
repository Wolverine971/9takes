# Who Takes Care Of You?

**Status:** queued
**Surface:** question page / Mirror Moment / Instagram Type 2 anchor
**Source:** [ENNEAGRAM_2_WORLDVIEW_BIBLE.md](../ENNEAGRAM_2_WORLDVIEW_BIBLE.md)

## Hook

You seem like the one who takes care of everyone. Who takes care of you?

## Core idea

Every group has the person who holds it together: remembers the birthdays, absorbs the crises, feeds the room. Almost nobody asks who holds them, because the performance of fineness is so good that people take it at face value. The question lands like a key in a lock: forward, intimate, and almost never asked. Nine types answer it in nine different registers, which makes it a perfect give-first seed.

## Structure

1. The person the question is for (the dissertation-vs-blank test).
2. Why nobody asks it (the fineness performance works too well).
3. What happens when someone finally does (the recalibration).
4. The give-first turn: answer it yourself before reading anyone else's.
