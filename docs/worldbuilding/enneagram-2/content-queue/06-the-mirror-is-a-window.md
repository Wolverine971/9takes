# The Mirror Is A Window With The Lights Off

**Status:** queued
**Surface:** growth explainer / carousel
**Source:** [ENNEAGRAM_2_WORLDVIEW_BIBLE.md](../ENNEAGRAM_2_WORLDVIEW_BIBLE.md)

## Hook

A one-way mirror is not a special kind of glass. It's ordinary glass with the lights off on one side.

## Core idea

The Two's master image, resolved by its own physics. The mirror stays a mirror only while the Two's side stays dark: needs unlit, interior unfindable, "I'm fine" as blackout curtains. The 2-to-4 growth movement is the lights coming on: naming needs, receiving without repaying, being seen instead of only seeing. Same glass, same room, same warmth. Light both sides and the watching becomes meeting.

## Structure

1. The booth (everyone else in HD, your side dark).
2. The physics (darkness is the entire trick).
3. Why the lights stay off (visible need feels like exposure).
4. The switch (small lights-on reps, listed).
5. The window (what mutual seeing actually feels like).

## Product tie-in

The canonical Type 2 growth visual for carousels: two-panel booth-then-window. Coaching prompt set: the lights-on reps.
