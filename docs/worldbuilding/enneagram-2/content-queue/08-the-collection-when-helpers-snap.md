# The Collection: When Helpers Snap

**Status:** queued
**Surface:** blog / relationship post
**Source:** [ENNEAGRAM_2_WORLDVIEW_BIBLE.md](../ENNEAGRAM_2_WORLDVIEW_BIBLE.md)

## Hook

The sweetest person you know has a due date.

## Core idea

The 2-to-8 stress move rendered through the ledger: years of unbilled care reaching collection at once. The warm one turns suddenly ferocious, demands retroactive acknowledgment, and then drowns in shame and over-corrects into even more giving, which restarts the cycle. Nobody saw the books, so nobody saw the audit coming. Includes the pre-snap spectrum (martyr performance, wounded withdrawal, sub-zero "I'm fine") so readers catch it earlier.

## Structure

1. The eruption everyone remembers (and the Two's shame after).
2. The books nobody saw.
3. The pre-snap spectrum, earliest tells first.
4. What the snap is actually saying (unexpressed needs choosing their own exit).
5. The repair question: what need have I been converting into favors?
