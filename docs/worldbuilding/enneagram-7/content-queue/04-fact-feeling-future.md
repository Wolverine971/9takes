# Fact, Feeling, Future

**Status:** queued
**Surface:** practical tool / social / relationship prompt
**Source:** [ENNEAGRAM_7_WORLDVIEW_BIBLE.md](../ENNEAGRAM_7_WORLDVIEW_BIBLE.md)

## Hook

Your reframe may be true. It may also be arriving too early.

## Core Idea

A healthy reframe includes reality instead of replacing it. Before finding the opportunity, name the unspun fact and let the feeling or cost register.

## Structure

1. **Fact:** What happened without interpretation?
2. **Feeling:** What did it cost, change, or stir?
3. **Future:** What possibility remains now?
4. Notice which step the mind wants to skip.
5. Use the reframe after contact, not instead of contact.

## Product Tie-in

Turn the three steps into a short response composer or prompt card below Type 7 questions.

## Guardrail

Do not frame optimism as fake. The issue is sequence and inclusion of negative data.
