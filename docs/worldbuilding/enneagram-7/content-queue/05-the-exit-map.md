# The Exit Map

**Status:** queued
**Surface:** product / question prompt / social
**Source:** [ENNEAGRAM_7_WORLDBUILDING_MAP.md](../ENNEAGRAM_7_WORLDBUILDING_MAP.md)

## Hook

Type 7s can map a way out before they consciously want to leave.

## Core Idea

Attention tracks backup plans, alternate identities, future openings, and foreclosed choices. This can be resourceful navigation or a refusal to inhabit the present, depending on whether the exit answers real evidence or only discomfort.

## Structure

1. The scan: routes, options, closures, better futures.
2. Why an available exit feels regulating.
3. What the map catches that others miss.
4. What it can misread as a cage.
5. The check: "Protecting my life, or protecting me from life here?"

## Product Tie-in

Question prompt: "What exit do you keep mapped even though you say you want to stay?"

## Guardrail

Do not moralize exits. Some routes out are healthy, necessary, and overdue.
