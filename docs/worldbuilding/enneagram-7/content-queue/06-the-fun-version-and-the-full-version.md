# The Fun Version And The Full Version

**Status:** queued
**Surface:** dating / relationships
**Source:** `docs/content-research/red-flags-dating-outline.md`

## Hook

The people who love a Type 7 do not only want the fun version. They want the version that stays when the room gets heavy.

## Core Idea

Seven energy can make a relationship adventurous and hopeful. It becomes lonely when pain is minimized, shared consequences are reframed away, or a partner must compete with imagined alternatives.

## Structure

1. Why Seven energy is attractive.
2. The difference between lifting the mood and leaving the person.
3. How future plans can bypass present repair.
4. Fully arriving without surrendering autonomy.
5. Partner language: "Sit with me before we solve it."

## Product Tie-in

Question page: "When do you need someone's optimism, and when do you need them to stay with what hurts?"

## Guardrail

Describe behavior and impact. Do not label ordinary conflict avoidance as manipulation or gaslighting without evidence.
