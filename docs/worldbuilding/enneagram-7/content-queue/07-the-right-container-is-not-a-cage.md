# The Right Container Is Not A Cage

**Status:** queued
**Surface:** work / success / self-sabotage
**Source:** `src/blog/enneagram/why-the-next-thing-wont-fix-it-type-7.md`

## Hook

You may not need a new idea. You may need a structure strong enough to hold the ideas you already have.

## Core Idea

The best container does not eliminate Type 7 range. It creates bounded variety, visible completion, and enough continuity for skill and reputation to compound.

## Structure

1. Why unrestricted possibility scatters effort.
2. The difference between bad constraint and useful form.
3. Portfolio, season, sprint, and platform as Seven-friendly containers.
4. How to preserve variety inside one mission.
5. Container audit: range, boundary, completion, recovery.

## Product Tie-in

Question prompt: "What structure would protect the part of your work you most want to finish?"

## Guardrail

Do not assume every career pivot is escape. Use outcomes, repeated timing, and stated motivation.
