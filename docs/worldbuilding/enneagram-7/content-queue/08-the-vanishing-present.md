# The Vanishing Present

**Status:** queued
**Surface:** blog / manifesto / carousel
**Source:** [ENNEAGRAM_7_WORLDBUILDING_MAP.md](../ENNEAGRAM_7_WORLDBUILDING_MAP.md)

## Hook

The Type 7 trap is not wanting more from life. It is making this life disappear while reaching for it.

## Core Idea

The Vanishing Present has two voices: "You are trapped here forever" and "Your real life is somewhere else." One triggers escape; the other ensures escape never produces arrival.

## Structure

1. The Cage: no choice, no movement, no future.
2. Permanent Elsewhere: satisfaction one option away.
3. How both voices make the present uninhabitable.
4. The correction: options can exist inside contact.
5. Committed joy: the freedom to stay without pretending there is no door.

## Product Tie-in

Close with a 9takes question: "What part of your life have you turned into a waiting room?"

## Guardrail

Do not make ambition, pleasure, future planning, or necessary escape the enemy. The target is automatic absence from the present.
