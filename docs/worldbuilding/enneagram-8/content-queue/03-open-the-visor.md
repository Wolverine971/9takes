# Open The Visor

**Status:** queued
**Surface:** carousel / coaching prompt
**Source:** [ENNEAGRAM_8_WORLDVIEW_BIBLE.md](../ENNEAGRAM_8_WORLDVIEW_BIBLE.md)

## Hook

Growth for Type 8 is not taking the armor off. It is learning when to open the visor.

## Core idea

Generic vulnerability language fails for Eights because it sounds like surrender. "Open the visor" preserves agency: chosen access, chosen person, chosen moment, chosen amount.

## Structure

1. Why "just be vulnerable" triggers resistance.
2. What the visor protects.
3. The first opening should be small.
4. Watch what happens.
5. Repeat with people who earn access.

## Product tie-in

Coaching prompt: name one trustworthy person and one small truth you could share without flooding the system.
