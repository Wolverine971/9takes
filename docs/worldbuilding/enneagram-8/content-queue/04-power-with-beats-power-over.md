**Status:** queued
**Surface:** leadership / self-sabotage blog

The Eight's lone-wolf strategy creates a ceiling. The breakthrough comes when power stops being only self-protection and becomes shared mission, alliance, delegation, and protection of others.

2. Why doing it alone caps scale.
