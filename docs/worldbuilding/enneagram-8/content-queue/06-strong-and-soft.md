# Strong And Soft

**Status:** queued
**Surface:** dating / relationship content
**Source:** `docs/content-research/red-flags-dating-outline.md`

## Hook

The people who love a Type 8 do not only want protection. They want access.

## Core idea

Eight strength is attractive because it feels safe. It becomes painful when protection turns into control and softness is treated like weakness.

## Structure

1. What draws people to Eights.
2. The safety/control inversion.
3. "Strong and soft" as the partner need.
4. What healthy protection looks like.
5. What controlling protection feels like.

## Product tie-in

Question page: "When do you feel safest with someone's strength, and when does it start to feel like control?"
