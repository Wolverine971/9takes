# The Power Map

**Status:** queued
**Surface:** product / question prompt / social
**Source:** [ENNEAGRAM_8_WORLDBUILDING_MAP.md](../ENNEAGRAM_8_WORLDBUILDING_MAP.md)

## Hook

Type 8s read the room before the room says anything.

## Core idea

The Eight's attention goes to power: who has it, who lacks it, who abuses it, who pretends, who folds, who needs protection. This can be social intelligence or paranoia depending on health.

## Structure

1. The scan: leverage, threat, loyalty, competence.
2. Why it exists.
3. What it catches that others miss.
4. What it misreads.
5. How to check the map before acting on it.

## Product tie-in

Question prompt: "What is the first power dynamic you notice when you enter a room?"
