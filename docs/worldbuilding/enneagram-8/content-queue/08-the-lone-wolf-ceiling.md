# The Lone-Wolf Ceiling

**Status:** queued
**Surface:** success / self-sabotage
**Source:** `docs/content-research/self-sabotage-research.md`, `src/blog/enneagram/how-type-8-challengers-actually-succeed.md`

## Hook

Working alone can prove you are strong. It can also keep you smaller than you need to be.

## Core idea

Type 8 self-reliance is powerful until scale requires trust. The unsupported Eight either succeeds smaller or fails alone, then calls that proof that people cannot be trusted.

2. The sabotage cycle.
3. The support withdrawal problem.
4. Collaboration as strategy, not weakness.
5. Help-before-crisis experiment.
