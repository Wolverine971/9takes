## 3. Biography timeline

**Childhood and roots**

- 1970-06-26 - born Joliet, Illinois; raised in Minooka (pop. ~2,000). Father Ric, social studies teacher; mother Cathy, nurse; raised Catholic, second of four (Wikipedia).
- 1970s-80s - childhood on grandparents' farms four miles apart; first wage a nickel for shoveling pig manure in grandpa Mike Roberts' barn (Paddle Your Own Canoe, 2013; Looper).

**Training and Chicago**

- 1988-1993 - BFA theater, University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (U of I News).
- ~1990-93 - kabuki under Shozo Sato: tea ceremony, ikebana, Zen; a year off school touring with the troupe; travels to Japan (U of I News; IPM Newsroom; Daily Illini).
- 1993-96 - co-founds Defiant Theatre, Chicago (stage combat, provocation); pays rent as a carpenter; dumpster lumber for sets; Goodman debut 1994 as fight captain; master carpenter at Steppenwolf/Goodman (Wikipedia; Block Club Chicago, 2026).

**Los Angeles, Megan, the woodshop**

- Late 1990s - moves to LA; small TV roles (ER, The West Wing, Will & Grace, 24, George Lopez, Deadwood) (Wikipedia).
- Summer 2000 - meets Megan Mullally in The Berlin Circle; her first impression: "not cute... there was a lot of back hair," then "Wait a minute - he's funny" (E! Online).
- 2001 - founds Offerman Woodshop, East LA (Lee Valley; offermanwoodshop.com).
- 2003-09-20 - surprise backyard wedding (Parade; Wikipedia).
- ~2007-08 - builds first canoe, Huckleberry, in a rented Red Hook shop; films it at Ted Moores' request; the 2008 DVD wins a paddling-film award (Paddling Magazine; Bear Mountain Boats).

**Parks and after**

- 2008 - auditions for Parks; NBC passes on his looks for "Josh"; Schur and Daniels counter with Ron Swanson; four months of auditions (Screen Rant; Yahoo Finance).
- 2008/09 - the offer call; sobs for 30 minutes (The New York Times, 2013 via Daily Actor). Age 38 at the April 9, 2009 premiere.
- 2009-2015 - Ron Swanson, 7 seasons; TCA Award 2011 (Wikipedia).
- 2013 - Paddle Your Own Canoe (Penguin Random House).
- 2014 - American Ham Netflix special; Lagavulin "My Tales of Whisky" partnership begins (Wikipedia; Forbes, 2025).
- 2015 - Gumption; Fargo season 2 (Penguin Random House; IMDb). December 2015: the Yule Log video (Adweek; Time).
- 2016 - Good Clean Fun; The Founder (Dick McDonald); co-produces Look & See: A Portrait of Wendell Berry - appears only as hands building a stool (Penguin Random House; Wikipedia; PBS Independent Lens).
- 2018 - The Greatest Love Story Ever Told with Mullally; the two-week rule goes public (Variety, 2018); Making It with Amy Poehler 2018-2021; Hearts Beat Loud (Wikipedia).
- 2019-20 - All Rise tour; Devs (Wikipedia; Daily Nebraskan).
- 2021 - Where the Deer and the Antelope Play: Glacier NP with George Saunders and Jeff Tweedy; James Rebanks' Cumbrian farm; Airstream West with Megan (Goodreads; Seattle Times).
- 2023 - The Last of Us "Long, Long Time" (Bill); Dumb Money (Ken Griffin) (Wikipedia).
- 2024 - Emmy win (Jan 6 ceremony); Spirit Awards speech vs homophobic backlash (Feb); Civil War (the President) (TheWrap; Variety; Wikipedia).
- 2025 - "I'm Not Ron Swanson" single (Jan); Sovereign (Jerry, sovereign-citizen father, July); Little Woodchucks with Lee Buchanan (Oct); Death by Lightning (Chester A. Arthur, Nov) (Spotify/Shazam; Wikipedia; Iowa Public Radio).
- 2026 - Big Woodchuck Tour (Feb + April); Good Hang episode (Apr 21); Variety awards profile pegged to Margo's Got Money Troubles (June 10, paywalled - role details from search summaries); Iceboy! at the Goodman opposite Mullally (June 29 - Aug 16, extended); upcoming Japan House fundraiser honoring Shozo Sato (Consequence; iHeart; Variety; Block Club Chicago; CBS Chicago; WCIA).
