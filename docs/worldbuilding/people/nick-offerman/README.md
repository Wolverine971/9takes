# Nick Offerman worldbuilding

The first person-level worldbuilding run for 9takes. Where `docs/worldbuilding/enneagram-1/` maps a type's worldview, this folder maps one subject's world: Nick Offerman, Enneagram Type 9 (9w1), subject of the 9takes personality-analysis blog. It applies the same 7-element workflow (`docs/worldbuilding/WORLDBUILDING_TEMPLATE.md`) to a real, sourced biography instead of a type doctrine - which means every lore beat, quote, and claim in this folder carries an outlet and a year.

## What this folder feeds

1. **The Nick Offerman people-draft blog** (`src/blog/people/drafts/Nick-Offerman.md`) - the draft is graded C with two caps (`originality_cross_draft_sameness`, `evidence_untraceable_quote`). The map supplies a diagnosis architecture built from Offerman's own material instead of the shared merge template, and the research notes supply the verified-quote fixes.
2. **/moodboard and /carousel person posts** - the image hooks, settings, and visual objects in the map are written to be renderable; feed them straight into the Story-mode brief.
3. **The Instagram Type 9 anchor post** - 9takes currently has NO published Type 9 IG anchor. This map is the seed: the type-pond strategy needs a Type 9 anchor, and Offerman is the strongest Type 9 subject on file.
4. **A future `docs/worldbuilding/enneagram-9/` type run** - no Type 9 worldview run exists yet (only 1, 2, 7, 8). The type-level material here (the merge mechanics, counterfeit peace, the presence-vs-numbness distinction) is raw material for that run, expressed through one well-sourced life.

## Bottom line

Offerman's world is not "the manliest man on television." It is the world of a Nine who found the healthy version of the type's core move.

The Type 9 talent is the merge: dissolving into something calmer and larger than your own ego. Offerman has done it all his life, on the record - a master's kabuki discipline, a marriage governed by a two-week rule, a character he still corrects people about, a workbench he calls medicine. The same mechanism, unpicked, buries the wanting: sixteen years of small parts and day jobs he called fine came out in one thirty-minute cry when the Parks and Recreation offer finally arrived (The New York Times, 2013).

The 9takes Offerman promise:

> Merging is not the problem. Merging with everything except your own life is. Pick your merges - then paddle your own canoe.

The growth frame that keeps the type's gift intact: below the line by choice is service; below the line by default is disappearance. Offerman is the exhibit that a Nine can step into the spotlight without becoming a different person - the stillness stays, the preferences finally get voiced.

Use this folder when writing the Offerman blog, Offerman social posts, the Type 9 IG anchor, Type 9 Mirror Moments, and (later) the enneagram-9 worldview run.
