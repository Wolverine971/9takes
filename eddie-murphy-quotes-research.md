# Eddie Murphy Quotes & Interview Research Compilation

## ON HIS CHILDHOOD / FATHER'S DEATH / FOSTER CARE

**On his very first memory:**
"My very first memory is my mother and father fighting -- she threw the Virgin Mary at him."
_Source: Being Eddie documentary (Netflix, 2025); also [Consequence of Sound recap](https://consequence.net/2025/11/being-eddie-eddie-murphy-revelations-netflix/)_

**On his father's murder:**
"One of those crimes of passion: 'If I can't have you, no one else will' kind of deal."
_Source: Being Eddie documentary; [Geo News](https://www.geo.tv/latest/633948-eddie-murphy-recalls-childhood-trauma-that-impacted-his-life)_

**On dim memories of his father:**
"My mother and father broke up when I was 3, and he died when I was 8, so I have very dim memories. Every now and then, I wonder what it would be like if he was still here."
_Source: Multiple interviews; [Distractify](https://www.distractify.com/p/eddie-murphy-parents)_

**On the impact of childhood trauma:**
"I'm sure it affected me a bunch of different ways. There was a lot of trauma, and we're shaped by that stuff."
_Source: Being Eddie documentary; [Distractify](https://www.distractify.com/p/eddie-murphy-parents)_

**On foster care:**
"Those were baaaaaad days. Staying with her was probably the reason I became a comedian."
_Source: Multiple interviews; [Distractify](https://www.distractify.com/p/eddie-murphy-parents); [More Than Our Childhoods](https://www.morethanourchildhoods.org/stories/eddie-murphy/)_

**On his stepfather Vernon Lynch:**
"By the grace of God, my mother married an amazing, solid man who put all the right s--t in me. That's crucial. It makes a huge difference."
_Source: Being Eddie documentary; [Distractify](https://www.distractify.com/p/eddie-murphy-parents)_

**On his overall childhood:**
"I had a great upbringing... Most of my childhood memories are fond. It was pretty normal kid stuff."
_Source: [More Than Our Childhoods](https://www.morethanourchildhoods.org/stories/eddie-murphy/)_

## ON COMEDY AND PERFORMING

**On his artistic identity:**
"I'm not a stand-up comedian. I'm funny, but I don't go, 'I'm a comedian,' like I don't go 'I'm an actor' or 'I'm a musician.' I'm an artist that can express himself a bunch of different ways."
_Source: Being Eddie documentary; [Consequence of Sound](https://consequence.net/2025/11/being-eddie-eddie-murphy-revelations-netflix/)_

**On being a supersensitive artist:**
"I'm a supersensitive artist, and I can express myself creatively in a bunch of different ways."
_Source: Multiple interviews; [A-Z Quotes](https://www.azquotes.com/author/10583-Eddie_Murphy)_

**On sensitivity and talent:**
"Sensitivity is the gauge, not how much talent you have. The most sensitive one will be the artist that's most in tune."
_Source: Being Eddie documentary; [Consequence of Sound](https://consequence.net/2025/11/being-eddie-eddie-murphy-revelations-netflix/)_

**On humor and perception:**
"Sense of humor is ultimately an acute sense of proportion. The funny person notices stuff first."
_Source: Being Eddie documentary; [E! Online](https://www.eonline.com/news/1424984/eddie-murphy-on-his-obsessive-compulsive-disorder)_

**On his creative process:**
"When I'm on camera or doing anything on mic, I just do it and when finished, it goes away. There is no process."
_Source: [A-Z Quotes](https://www.azquotes.com/author/10583-Eddie_Murphy/tag/new_quotes_from_interviews)_

**On creative inspiration:**
"The creative spot is from God -- You hear voices, everybody does. When you get older, you refer to it as intuition."
_Source: [BrainyQuote](https://www.brainyquote.com/authors/eddie-murphy-quotes)_

**On performing evolving into self-revelation:**
"After a really long time as an artist, performance stops. I reveal myself to audiences now -- it's not a show anymore."
_Source: [A-Z Quotes](https://www.azquotes.com/author/10583-Eddie_Murphy/tag/new_quotes_from_interviews)_

**On stand-up dependency:**
"Doing stand-up means depending on myself. I've trusted my instincts since my teens and let material come from the top of my head."
_Source: [A-Z Quotes](https://www.azquotes.com/author/10583-Eddie_Murphy/tag/new_quotes_from_interviews)_

**On why he stopped doing stand-up:**
"I stopped doing stand-up because it stopped being fun."
_Source: [E! Online](https://www.eonline.com/news/789067/eddie-murphy-doesn-t-like-newspapers-e-mails-or-drugs-and-other-things-we-learned)_

**On comedy getting harder with fame:**
"It was harder to write... new stuff. It had gotten so crazy."
_Source: [E! Online](https://www.eonline.com/news/789067/eddie-murphy-doesn-t-like-newspapers-e-mails-or-drugs-and-other-things-we-learned)_

**On the relationship between comedy and money:**
(Jerry Seinfeld, discussing Eddie's situation): "When the money get bigger, and the fun gets smaller... Comedy and money, to me, are antithetical."
_Source: Comedians in Cars Getting Coffee (Netflix, 2019); [Variety](https://variety.com/2019/scene/news/jerry-seinfeld-eddie-murphy-comedians-cars-getting-coffee-comedy-1203271820/)_

**On his early performing instincts:**
"I've known I wanted to do this ever since I was a little kid and I used to get in trouble at church for goofing off all the time: mocking the preacher, imitating people and the things they did."
_Source: [BrainyQuote](https://www.brainyquote.com/authors/eddie-murphy-quotes)_

**On Richard Pryor:**
"The greatest comedian of all time."
_Source: Being Eddie documentary; [Media Play News review](https://www.mediaplaynews.com/being-eddie-review/)_

**On his brother Charlie Murphy:**
"Charlie was the funniest person alive."
_Source: Being Eddie documentary; [Media Play News review](https://www.mediaplaynews.com/being-eddie-review/)_

"There's nobody like Charlie." / "Yeah, I miss my brother."
_Source: Being Eddie documentary; [The Wrap](https://www.thewrap.com/being-eddie-netflix-recap-eddie-murphy-documentary/)_

"I've been doing him since I was two or three." (referring to impressions of Charlie)
_Source: [Distractify](https://www.distractify.com/p/eddie-murphy-parents)_

**On cancel culture and comedy:**
"I can't think of a comedian that said a joke that made them get canceled. I feel like that whole canceled thing was just a moment in time where people were like, 'Comics have to be careful what they say.'"
_Source: [Hollywood Reporter](https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/movies/movie-news/eddie-murphy-netflix-documentary-being-eddie-interview-1236419682/)_

**On critics:**
"Critics have never liked anything I've done. It's audience reaction that matters, not what critics know."
_Source: [A-Z Quotes](https://www.azquotes.com/author/10583-Eddie_Murphy/tag/new_quotes_from_interviews)_

## ON FAME AND PRIVACY

**On being a homebody, not a recluse:**
"I leave my house all the time! But I'm not at all the Hollywood parties. I'm grown, and where else am I supposed to be?"
_Source: [Today/NBC](https://www.today.com/news/eddie-murphy-im-homebody-not-recluse-wbna45062685); [TheGrio](https://thegrio.com/2011/11/03/eddie-murphy-im-a-homebody-not-a-recluse/)_

**On not doing the Hollywood circuit:**
"I don't do the Hollywood stuff. I've never been on the circuit."
_Source: [Today/NBC](https://www.today.com/news/eddie-murphy-im-homebody-not-recluse-wbna45062685)_

**On technology and media avoidance:**
"I don't have a computer, I don't have email -- I don't have any of that."
_Source: [E! Online](https://www.eonline.com/news/789067/eddie-murphy-doesn-t-like-newspapers-e-mails-or-drugs-and-other-things-we-learned)_

"I haven't read a newspaper in 20 years, or read a corporate magazine."
_Source: [E! Online](https://www.eonline.com/news/789067/eddie-murphy-doesn-t-like-newspapers-e-mails-or-drugs-and-other-things-we-learned)_

"If there's an article about me, someone has to read through it before they give it."
_Source: [E! Online](https://www.eonline.com/news/789067/eddie-murphy-doesn-t-like-newspapers-e-mails-or-drugs-and-other-things-we-learned)_

**On cautionary tales of fame:**
"Those guys are all cautionary tales for me." (referring to Elvis, Michael Jackson, Prince)
_Source: NYT "The Interview" podcast (July 2024); [Billboard](https://www.billboard.com/culture/tv-film/eddie-murphy-michael-jackson-prince-cautionary-tales-1235723715/)_

**On surviving fame as a young Black artist:**
"When you get famous really young, especially a black artist, it's like living in a minefield. Any moment, you can step on a mine. Any moment something can happen that can undo everything."
_Source: NYT "The Interview" podcast (2024); [Fox News](https://www.foxnews.com/entertainment/eddie-murphy-turned-down-drugs-robin-williams-john-belushi-god-looking-over-me)_

"And now, at this age, I can look back and be like, 'Wow, I came through a minefield [over] 35 years.' How do you make it through a minefield for 35, 40 years? Something has to be looking over you."
_Source: NYT "The Interview" podcast (2024); [Fox News](https://www.foxnews.com/entertainment/eddie-murphy-turned-down-drugs-robin-williams-john-belushi-god-looking-over-me)_

**On being the only young Black person on SNL:**
"I was the only Black person up there, and I was the only teenager up there."
_Source: Being Eddie documentary; [Biography.com](https://www.biography.com/celebrities/a69314987/eddie-murphy-saved-saturday-night-live-1980s)_

**On why his stardom exploded:**
"My stuff took off the way it took off because they'd never seen a young Black person go take charge in the white world."
_Source: Being Eddie documentary; [Consequence of Sound](https://consequence.net/2025/11/being-eddie-eddie-murphy-revelations-netflix/)_

**On race in his career:**
"In terms of my work and my career, race has never been an issue. I've been making movies for 40 years and never once could I not get a movie made because I was black."
_Source: Radio Times interview (2021); [Irish News](https://www.irishnews.com/magazine/entertainment/2021/03/02/news/eddie-murphy-says-race-has-never-been-an-issue-during-his-career-2240740/)_

"I transcended that stuff. But that's not to say I walked out of heaven and into Hollywood. I'm a black man who was born in America; I'm African American."
_Source: Radio Times interview (2021)_

**On racist press in the 1980s:**
"The press being relentless on me, and a lot of it was racist stuff... I was the only one out there. I'm this young, rich, Black one. Everybody wasn't happy about that in 1983."
_Source: Multiple interviews_

**On Hollywood power structures:**
"White men run this business. It's always been this way."
_Source: [WION News](https://www.wionews.com/entertainment/hollywood/news-eddie-murphy-says-white-men-run-the-business-in-hollywood-its-always-been-this-way-367532)_

**On audience reaction vs. personal ego:**
"People aren't reacting to me, Eddie Murphy. They're reacting to luck and the God in me."
_Source: [A-Z Quotes](https://www.azquotes.com/author/10583-Eddie_Murphy)_

**On his unique accomplishments:**
"There are things that I've done that nobody else could do."
_Source: [Hollywood Reporter](https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/movies/movie-news/eddie-murphy-netflix-documentary-being-eddie-interview-1236419682/)_

## ON FATHERHOOD AND FAMILY

**On his children being the center:**
"My career, or what I am as an artist, that's not at the center of my life. At the center of my life is my family and my kids. That's the principal relationship, and everything comes after that."
_Source: Vanity Fair (January 2020)_

**On his shift in priorities:**
"There was a time when I was at the center of everything, what I was doing, and how funny I was and how popular. I'm not at the center. Now my kids are and everything revolves around them."
_Source: New York Times (September 2019)_

**On his children's character:**
"I am so blessed with my kids. I don't have one bad seed. My kids are so great, normal people -- and nobody is like the Hollywood jerk kid."
_Source: [TheGrio](https://thegrio.com/2021/03/16/eddie-murphy-dad-of-10/); [Yahoo](https://www.yahoo.com/entertainment/eddie-murphy-loves-being-dad-151609780.html)_

**On loving fatherhood:**
"I love fatherhood."
_Source: [MovieGuide](https://www.movieguide.org/news-articles/eddie-murphy-i-love-fatherhood.html)_

**On being present:**
"My superpower is that I am always, always present. Talk to one of my kids anytime of day and ask, 'Where's your dad right now?' and they can look at their watch and tell you literally what part of the house I'm in. Dad is always present and always has been."
_Source: People magazine; [Geo News](https://www.geo.tv/latest/634045-eddie-murphy-on-parenting-superpower-i-am-always-present)_

**On parenting philosophy:**
"If you put your children first you never make a bad decision."
_Source: [More Than Our Childhoods](https://www.morethanourchildhoods.org/stories/eddie-murphy/)_

**On parenting being natural:**
Parenting requires no effort because "you're wired to do the right thing by your child."
_Source: Today show appearance with Pete Davidson; [Parade](https://parade.com/news/father-of-10-eddie-murphy-64-gave-pete-davidson-ultimate-effortless-parenting-advice)_

**On kids and balance:**
"I have 10 kids, and I'm present, and I'm part of their lives. You got to have some kind of balance with career and personal life."
_Source: Multiple interviews_

**On his kids' accomplishments:**
"My kids are smart and are trying to do stuff. I am blessed with my kids. I really, really got lucky."
_Source: [More Than Our Childhoods](https://www.morethanourchildhoods.org/stories/eddie-murphy/)_

**On his grandchild:**
"There's no sweeter blessing than seeing your grandchild."
_Source: Vanity Fair (January 2020)_

**On the SNL monologue about family:**
"If you would have told me 30 years ago that I would be this boring stay at home, you know, father of 10 house dad and Bill Cosby would be in jail, I would have took that bet."
_Source: SNL Monologue (December 2019)_

## ON HIS CAREER VALLEYS AND COMEBACK

**On making bad movies:**
"I was making these s----- movies. I was like, 'This s--- ain't fun. They're giving me Razzies.'"
_Source: WTF with Marc Maron podcast (March 2021); [Today](https://www.today.com/popculture/eddie-murphy-reveals-why-he-stopped-making-movies-t211190)_

**On the worst Razzie:**
"Motherf------ gave me the 'worst actor ever' Razzie. It was like, 'Maybe it's time to take a break.'"
_Source: WTF with Marc Maron (2021); [Today](https://www.today.com/popculture/eddie-murphy-reveals-why-he-stopped-making-movies-t211190)_

**On his extended hiatus:**
"I was only gonna take a break for a year, then all of a sudden six years go by, and I'm sitting on the couch, and I'm like, I kinda could sit on this couch and not get off it."
_Source: WTF with Marc Maron (2021); [Today](https://www.today.com/popculture/eddie-murphy-reveals-why-he-stopped-making-movies-t211190)_

**On motivating himself to come back:**
"I don't want to leave it the last bunch of s--- they see me do is bull----, so let me get off the couch and do some stuff and remind them that I'm funny."

**On his comeback strategy:**
"The plan was to go do 'Dolemite,' 'Saturday Night Live,' do 'Coming 2 America,' and then do stand-up and then see how I felt afterwards."

**On choosing Dolemite for the comeback:**
"I didn't want to just pop back up. I wanted a funny movie to remind them that they liked me. This movie turned out so strong that I figured this is a great way to come back."

**On viewing the comeback as a bookend:**
"I'm kind of looking at this period as a bookend. I hadn't been back to 'SNL.' Let's fix that. Let's do standup again."

**On defending Norbit:**
"I like 'Norbit.' To this day, I like 'Norbit.' There's stuff in 'Norbit' that makes me laugh."

## ON HIS PERSONALITY / SENSITIVITY / OCD / SELF-LOVE

**On self-love as his biggest blessing:**
"My biggest blessing is not my comedic talent -- my biggest blessing is that I love myself and I knew what I wanted to do really, really early."
_Source: Being Eddie documentary; [Consequence of Sound](https://consequence.net/2025/11/being-eddie-eddie-murphy-revelations-netflix/)_

**On self-love keeping him safe:**
"I love myself. Always loved myself. That's the most important thing, that's one of the reasons why I didn't destroy myself... I had all the temptations and all the madness and all the stuff that anybody has around me, but I always loved myself."
_Source: Being Eddie documentary; [Netflix Tudum](https://www.netflix.com/tudum/articles/being-eddie-documentary-recap)_

**On getting softer with age:**
"As you get older, you get different, and I'm a mushier, softer person as I get older."
_Source: Multiple interviews; [Hollywood Reporter](https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/movies/movie-news/eddie-murphy-netflix-documentary-being-eddie-interview-1236419682/)_

**On being comfortable in his skin:**
"I am the most comfortable I've ever been in my skin. I feel great and optimistic and totally comfortable being Eddie."
_Source: The Oprah Conversation (April 2021); [Yardbarker](https://www.yardbarker.com/entertainment/articles/eddie_murphy_opens_up_to_oprah_im_the_most_comfortable_ive_ever_been_in_my_own_skin/s1_13132_34582469)_

**On confidence:**
"I've always had confidence. It came because I have lots of initiative. I wanted to make something of myself."
_Source: [BrainyQuote](https://www.brainyquote.com/authors/eddie-murphy-quotes)_

**On OCD as a child:**
Murphy revealed he used to have OCD when he was a kid, describing rituals like checking his stove's gas dials every night, sometimes multiple times over the course of an hour. His mother and nobody knew this was going on.
_Source: Being Eddie documentary; [Netflix Tudum](https://www.netflix.com/tudum/articles/being-eddie-documentary-recap)_

**On forcing himself to stop OCD behavior:**
"I made myself stop doing it. 'Mental illness my ass.' I forced myself to stop doing it."
_Source: Being Eddie documentary; [The Wrap](https://www.thewrap.com/being-eddie-netflix-recap-eddie-murphy-documentary/)_

**On still managing OCD tendencies:**
He still checks the gas every night, but every now and then checks it twice, and coaches his internal thoughts: "Nah, motherfucker, you ain't starting that shit again."
_Source: Being Eddie documentary; multiple recaps_

**On OCD connecting to comedy:**
Murphy suggests that the sharp sense of detail in his comedy might be linked to compulsive behaviors carried since childhood.
_Source: Being Eddie documentary; [E! Online](https://www.eonline.com/news/1424984/eddie-murphy-on-his-obsessive-compulsive-disorder)_

**On early ambition:**
"Around 15, I started saying, 'When I'm 18, I'm gonna get famous.'"
_Source: [More Than Our Childhoods](https://www.morethanourchildhoods.org/stories/eddie-murphy/)_

"I knew I was going to be famous -- I was the funniest kid, always."
_Source: Being Eddie documentary; [Geo News](https://www.geo.tv/latest/633948-eddie-murphy-recalls-childhood-trauma-that-impacted-his-life)_

**On sobriety and drug avoidance:**
"I've never even tried cocaine or touched cocaine or some shit like that. I don't drink, I don't smoke cigarettes. I was 30 years old when I first smoked a joint."
_Source: Being Eddie documentary; [Consequence of Sound](https://consequence.net/2025/11/being-eddie-eddie-murphy-revelations-netflix/)_

**On turning down cocaine from Robin Williams and John Belushi at 19:**
"Over the years, I trip about that moment because I was really young, and it was so easy to try some coke. I wasn't taking some moral stance. I just wasn't interested in it."
_Source: NYT "The Interview" podcast (2024); [Fox News](https://www.foxnews.com/entertainment/eddie-murphy-turned-down-drugs-robin-williams-john-belushi-god-looking-over-me)_

"To not have the desire, the curiosity of it, I'd say that's providence. God was looking over me in that moment, I didn't make a left turn. Everything would have been different."
_Source: NYT "The Interview" podcast (2024); [Fox News](https://www.foxnews.com/entertainment/eddie-murphy-turned-down-drugs-robin-williams-john-belushi-god-looking-over-me)_

**On his position in life:**
"After all these years, I've done well and I'm cool. I feel comfortable in my skin, I've saved some paper, everybody's healthy, my kids are beautiful and smart, doing different things, it's all good."
_Source: Multiple interviews_

**On not letting praise or criticism sway him:**
"You can't let good things people say make you feel too good, because bad things will make you feel bad."
_Source: [A-Z Quotes](https://www.azquotes.com/author/10583-Eddie_Murphy/tag/new_quotes_from_interviews)_

**On divorce:**
"Getting divorced didn't sour me on the institution of marriage. I'll tell you what I'll never do. I'll never get divorced again."
_Source: [ThyBlackMan](https://thyblackman.com/2025/11/18/eddie-murphy-best-quotes-explained/)_

**On personal advice:**
"The advice I would give to someone is to not take anyone's advice."
_Source: [ThyBlackMan](https://thyblackman.com/2025/11/18/eddie-murphy-best-quotes-explained/)_

## ON RELATIONSHIPS WITH OTHER COMEDIANS / QUOTES FROM OTHERS ABOUT EDDIE

### Jerry Seinfeld on Eddie Murphy:

"What happened to you, happens to nobody. Your fame and your power and your intensity was so explosive [that] it scared everybody, because nobody had ever seen anything like that happen before or since really... That kind of thing only happens once every 50 years, 100 years. It's very, very rare."
_Source: Paley Center panel (July 2019); [Variety](https://variety.com/2019/scene/news/jerry-seinfeld-eddie-murphy-comedians-cars-getting-coffee-comedy-1203271820/)_

"To me, that's the fun thing... just to be with him for the day. That's why I do [the show]. There's an air in the room with comedians, right? The air changes... That was the most addictive thing to me about entering this world."
_Source: Comedians in Cars Getting Coffee (2019)_

### Eddie Murphy on Seinfeld:

"[Seinfeld] was always the funniest. He had his show down, he was polished, he had it all down before everybody. You had it together as a person, too, you were the first comic that had a nice car. We were all standing outside like, 'Look at Seinfeld's Saab! He bought this with jokes!' That was amazing to us."
_Source: Comedians in Cars Getting Coffee (2019); [Variety](https://variety.com/2019/scene/news/jerry-seinfeld-eddie-murphy-comedians-cars-getting-coffee-comedy-1203271820/)_

## ON SNL AND THE DAVID SPADE FEUD

**On the David Spade joke hurting him:**
"I was hurt, my feelings were hurt."
_Source: Being Eddie documentary; [The Wrap](https://www.thewrap.com/being-eddie-netflix-recap-eddie-murphy-documentary/)_

**On feeling the joke was personal:**
"This is Saturday Night Live. I'm the biggest thing that ever came off that show. The show would have been off the air if I didn't go back on the show, and now you got somebody from the cast making a crack about my career? And I know that [Spade] can't just say that. A joke has to go through these channels. So the producers thought it was OK to say that."
_Source: [Biography.com](https://www.biography.com/actors/a69364302/eddie-murphy-saturday-night-live-joke-feud-history)_

**On feeling it was racist:**
Murphy "felt it was racist."
_Source: [Biography.com](https://www.biography.com/actors/a69364302/eddie-murphy-saturday-night-live-joke-feud-history)_

**On being over it now:**
"That little friction I had with 'SNL' was 35 years ago. I don't have no smoke with no David Spade."
_Source: Being Eddie documentary; [The Wrap](https://www.thewrap.com/being-eddie-netflix-recap-eddie-murphy-documentary/)_

"I'm cool with everybody. It's all love."
