Pedro Pascal is a Type 6 (Loyalist)—the anxious protector whose warmth comes from genuine worry about people he loves. Jacob Elordi is a Type 4 (Individualist)—the brooding artist who resents being reduced to his looks. Timothee Chalamet is a Type 6—the sensitive prodigy whose vulnerability reads as authenticity.
