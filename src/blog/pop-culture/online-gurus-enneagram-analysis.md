Alex Hormozi is a Type 3 (Achiever) who turned business education into a personal brand empire—his content IS his product. Gary Vee is a Type 3 who performs hustle culture so relentlessly it's become its own philosophy. Ali Abdaal is a Type 3 who repackaged productivity as lifestyle content.
