# 9takes automated content use policy

Effective: 2026-07-17
Site: https://9takes.com
Machine-readable license: https://9takes.com/license.xml

Allowed without a separate license:

- traditional web-search indexing, with links and short excerpts;
- social and messaging link previews;
- retrieval initiated by a person using an AI assistant;
- AI search and answer grounding that clearly attributes 9takes and includes a functional
  link to the source page.

Not allowed without prior written permission:

- training, fine-tuning, evaluating, or developing an AI or machine-learning model using
  9takes content;
- building or refreshing a dataset, corpus, knowledge base, or substitute publication
  from a substantial collection of 9takes content;
- bulk copying, republishing, or redistributing 9takes articles or personality analyses;
- evading technical restrictions or misrepresenting an automated client as a person.

Access to the site does not grant rights beyond this policy. Copyright and other rights
are reserved. The canonical source URL should remain attached to every permitted excerpt
or grounded answer.
