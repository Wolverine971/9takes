# 9takes — Nine Inner Tensions carousel v2

Post-ready 13-slide Instagram carousel reframing the Enneagram passions as nine recurring inner tensions.

## Narrative

1. Preserve the approved “One mask. Nine inner tensions.” review board as the cover.
2. Use the seven deadly sins as a familiar doorway into the nine passions.
3. Name Deceit and Fear as the two additional patterns, then define passion and virtue.
4. Give each Enneagram type one focused passion-to-virtue slide.
5. Close with a personal reflection prompt.

## Files

- `carousel/slide-01-cover.png` through `carousel/slide-13-closing-question.png` — 1080 × 1350 PNG exports.
- `carousel/caption-and-alt-text.md` — post copy, framework note, and per-slide accessibility text.
- `9takes-nine-inner-tensions-carousel-v2-contact-sheet.png` — full sequence review.
- `9takes-nine-inner-tensions-instagram-carousel-v2.zip` — upload package.
- `build-carousel-v2.mjs` — deterministic Sharp compositor.

## Content choices

- “Avarice / Greed” is used for Type 5 to pair the traditional term with familiar language.
- Deceit is framed primarily as self-deception and performance, not simple lying.
- Fear is framed as attention organizing around uncertainty and trust, not only feeling scared.
- The seven-deadly-sins comparison is used as a reader-friendly analogy; it does not claim the two systems have identical historical origins.

No live site, app icon, or production logo assets were changed.
