## Executive Summary

This episode starts as a reaction to Elon interview dynamics (especially technical pushback from the host), then pivots into a founder playbook:

- hire for exceptional proof, not resume polish,
- run with aggressive urgency,
- identify and attack the current bottleneck,
- protect attention as a scarce strategic resource.

The back half reframes focus loss as a societal and business opportunity (measurement, training, and services for attention hygiene).

   Focus is treated like health: measurable, trainable, and commercially serviceable.
