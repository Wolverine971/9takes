A central tension in the episode is simultaneous declaration of control and visible signs of overactivation. This contradiction is not failure by itself, but it is a risk indicator for relapse cycles in public creators.

### 7) Useful Recovery Principle That Survives the Noise

### 8) Practical Takeaways
